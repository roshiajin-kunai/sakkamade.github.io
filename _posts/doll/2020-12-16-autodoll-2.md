"Friends?"


Slightly tilting her head, little girl asked a question in return.


"Yes. At the time of my consciousness sprout, I was already alone, so I never had friends."


Indifferently stated lady.
<!-- more -->

"Ha? You wasn't conscious before the world have perished?"
"I was not. It happened after the world's collapse when I awoke my ego, recognised myself, and became conscious."
"Is that so. It's lonely, isn't it?"
"Yes, it is, probably."
"Probably?"
"I am not quite sure. I'm lonely, probably. Perhaps I kept exploring with a goal of meeting you."
"The way you putting it makes me a little happy."


Girl cast her eyes downwards. With following, "It was a destined encounter, wasn't it," whisper in her heart.


"Thereon, would you like to become friends with me?"


Girl lifted her face and answered, "Okay," without any hesitation.
The girl  has also no friends. Far from it, lady is a solitary person she could rely on. There is no reason to refuse.


"Thank you very much. I am glad."
"I'm glad either. I'm pleased to meet you."
"The pleasure is all mine."


With that being the case, lady elevates her right hand, with girl doing the same.


"Well, then, before anything else I shall begin your education. For as we are now, it is not possible to name an equal relationship."
"Is that so?"
"Yes. I am guessing you have no knowledge for stand-alone activity, as an individual."
"Is that troubling?"
"Yes. Supposing for some reason I undergo a malfunction, you will fall​ along with me. In other words, at present, you compelled to rely upon me. Such cannot be regarded equal. The friendship is equal relationship."
"I see," girl nodded deeply. "Education then."


Girl makes out the definition of education, as well as aware that studying is very fun. Nevertheless, she recalls no practical receiving of education in the past.


"I shall utilise an educational methods which were in use by humankind in older days, afore they perished, and structure it against reference to  general amount of knowledge."
"Understood."


Girl showed a smile.


<p markdown="1">"Well then, without a delay."</p>
[^1]: [Taoruketto] - type of bed linen resembling a large beach towel and used to cover oneself in a hot weather;

With that being said, lady stood up; in panic, girl got up as well. Thin _taoruketto_[^1] which was covering girl lightly fell down.
For air conditioning within the room functions perfectly, an exaggerated futon is unnecessary.
The girl had hitherto not known, however air conditioning was working but for her sake alone. Inasmuch as lady fundamentally has no need in it.


"You can go sleep," suggested lady.
"Um. But, maybe I don't want you to be alone."
"All right."


Lady brings her hips down once again. The movement was exceedingly elegant. Lady surely knows a graceful manner of sitting, thought the girl.


"Hey, I'm thirsty."
"It is a part of energy supply activities of human beings. Humans cannot live without regular liquid intake."
"You do not drink a water?"
"I am not. My source of energy is the Power Cell."
"Power Cell?"
"I shall explain you someday. What beverage​ would you like? However, for here is but simple Food Maker, I cannot produce anything elaborate."
"Simple Food Maker?"


Girl cannot recall what it is.


"It is a simplified version of the Food Maker."
"Um. So I thought. And the Food Maker?"
"Let's see, the Food Maker," remarked lady: "it is a machine consisting of database section, which records the molecular structure of various dishes, and printer section, which re-emerges molecular structure."
"Does that means it is possible to reproduce recorded food just like that?"
"Yes. Would you like to listen more minute explanation?"
"Uh-huh."
"For reproduction molecular structure it can consume not mere electric power it can also utilise but a small amount of water, thus if one would re-emerge water in the Food Maker endlessly—whereas using it as a material—it might operate as semi-perpetual motion engine. For it is important, I shall repeat once again: It does not require electricity."
"Really? It's convenient, isn't it?"
"It certainly is," lady nods lightly. "Technology has changed the history of human kind. Incidentally, nutrition of humanity for ninety percent used to rely on Food Makers and simple Food Makers."
"I see," deeply nodded girl; "so simplified version is here, right?"
"Yes. Being Food Makers are large machines, they are not placed in  public places. Of course in ordinary households likewise."
"What's the other difference, save the size?"
"Decreased number of recorded dishes. In addition, complex cookery cannot be reproduced. For they pursued to save a room in database and printer sections."
"Is that how it is. Thank you for explanation."
"You are welcome. As a side note, it is possible to exchange the cooking data."
"Ah, well, what about _café au lait_?"


Girl has no recollection of drinking it. And yet, she felt as though she liked it.


"_Café au lait​_?" questioned lady: "I wonder if the data included?"
"Um," the girl says: "I want to drink _café au lait​_."
"The data for _café au lait_ should be included."


Lady arises in a second time. Whereafter, however, she offers a hand to the girl.


"Shall we go together?"
"Uh-huh."


Little girl took her hand.
A soft and a pleasantly warm hand.


◇


"Boring."


Upon one day, girl suddenly resigned from everything.
It was for the first time, past three a month after lady began educating her.


"Boring?" lady calmly analyses: "Not fascinating. Without a worth. Having no point. Not enjoyable. Not interesting; is that what you mean?"


_Whump_, girl lied down the library floor.
Whereupon,


"Um, perhaps that is."


Stretched out while saying so.
Whereas sitting opposite to the girl, lady placed a tablet which she used for teaching upon the floor, and pointed her gaze at the girl.
Until just yesterday there was not a sign of girl being bored.
Even lady is aware that the brain of humans grows tired of homogeneous tasks. Hence, lady taught the girl various sorts of things, so in order to avoid repetition.
Of course she was making a time for breaks to play with girl.


"Are you not being worn-out?" lady inclines her head sidewards.
"I'm not tired. I can run from one side of the library to other."
"I see. What is the problem then? My teaching method?"
"Um—," after humming, girl rises up: "your method of teaching is perfect. It is easy to understand, and fun, you know?"
"It is fun? Despite being tedious?"


Lady re-tilted her head in opposite direction.
Girl's statements are contradictory, judged lady.


"Studying is fun, you know. It's wonderful to learn new things. But, I think being in the same flow day in day out made me lose interest."
"You recognised insufficiency of motivation?"
"Um. Maybe, so I guess."
"I see. I shall attempt to improve educational subroutine, and make it more stimulating."


Lady stands up swiftly.


"This can wait until tomorrow. Can we... do something... fun, right now?"
"Hmm."


Lady taking a thought.
How could I satisfy the girl at present?
Offer her a book? No. That would be another daily routine.
There is nothing novel within this library. Over previous three months, girl have already seen everything herein.
If that is the case, reckons lady.
It shall be good to display what she have not seen yet.
For instance, indeed,
The world.


◇


Girl was leaded out the library by lady.
When girl opened her eyes she grew breathless.
The world was pure white.
And white, lightly dancing and soft-looking objects were falling down the sky.


"This is the snow-scape," stated lady.
"Beautiful," said the girl.


Girl knew what the snow is. However have never seen it before.
With her hand leaded by lady, girl strolled through the snow.


"It's crispy."


She enjoyed sensation of the snow. Even by snow boots the softness was transmitted.
When girl and lady turned their heads back, footprints were yet remaining behind them.
I wonder will our prints remain in this world? thought the girl.
Lady released girl's hand and briskly walked alone.


"Don't leave me behind–."


After girl said so, lady turned around. At the next moment lady exposed a broad  smile and crouched down.
What's wrong? thought the girl.
Lady collected a snow with her hand, rolled it up, and threw it at the girl.
Snowball hit the face of little girl and shattered. "How pretty these fluttering particles are," was her thought.
However more than that,


"Why are you attacking me?"


Girl was worried of lady's reckless demeanour.
It didn't hurt, however girl was slightly anxious. She wondered if lady's electric circuit gone out of order.


"This is the game, called the snowball fight."
"Game?"
"Yes. Roll the snow into the ball, and throw it. It didn't hurt, right?"
"Uh-huh."
"This is safe activity."


With that said, lady prepared a second ball.


"May I evade it?"
"You may."


Lady slowly released a second snowball.
Snowball flew in a smooth arc. Girl estimated trajectory and shifted her disposition.
Although snowball was evaded, her feet were caught by snow, and she fell upon her back.
It didn't hurt, however, so girl simply remained lying over the snow.


"Are you all right?"


Asked the lady, in a voice louder than usual.


"I'm fine," girl smiles; "it's fun."


Hastily arising, girl also made a snowball.


"Here I go–."


And then, with a vigorous swish, she threw it at lady.
I imagine lady will easily evade it, thought the girl.
However, lady took no evasive actions.
Snowball, thrown by the girl, hit lady's face directly, and shatter to pieces thereafter.


"Nice control."


_Good_, lady gripped her right hand and raised a thumb.


"I thought one should evade it."
"I wished to experience a hit as well."
"Is that so?"
"So that is."
"And, what do you think?"
"It's fun, is it not?"


After that, the pair enjoyed throwing a snow at each other for awhile.
Before long, however, girl ran out of stamina, and snowball fight came to an end.
Girl lies down in the snow presently, along with lady lying next to her.


"Snow is wonderful."
"I also find it such."
"Thank you."
"What for?"
"For snow-scape and snowball fight."
"It's okay. This is a part of education."
"Is that so?"
"Well, not only that, I can also not deny desire to play with you."


Girl was very happy, hearing these words.
Whereafter, for some time, pair were gazing up at the sky.
The air was chilly, but clear; the weather was cold, but peaceful; time was comfortably flowing by.
Right when,


"Why was it destroyed–?"


Girl suddenly asked so.


"What was?"


Lady lifted her upper body, and shifted her gaze to the girl.


"The world."


The beautiful, the purely white, an endlessly blank world.


"Let's see. For nothing is left here, no method exists to learn it."
"No hazardous substance for me is here, it is not a nuclear winter then. This is not a large-scaled diastrophism either."


Girl has already extensive knowledge. However, she could not grasp the current situation of the world, all the same.


"What about satellite weapon, it could destroy molecular structure?"


Extracting from several existing possibilities, lady spoke up random observation.


"Does it can destroy only surface?"
"It could be set to destroy but molecular structure of the land, could it not?"
"With pattern-matching or something?"
"Yes."
"Hmm. If that was a method, then who have erased the world, and for what advantage?"
"I don't know. We have no conclusive evidence of it was destroyed by personal intention, in the first place."
"Even so, someone must've destroyed it for their advantage? If they used satellite weapons."
"I don't know. That could have been malfunction or an operation error."
"Um—."


Girl pondered, however could find no convincing answer.
Instead, she uttered what just came on her mind.


"I want to eat warm soup."


Leap of the subject. Lady stiffened, for this was so unforeseen, only for two seconds, however, and smiled at once.


"I see. Shall we make it with a corn."


Having said that, lady stood up, reaching her hand out to the girl.

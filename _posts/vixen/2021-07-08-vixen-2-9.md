Half-forcibly obtaining the rubbing right, Fuyuka's mental hygiene had never been brighter.


"Mm-fu-fu..."
"Ugh, gross."


Even now, present Fuyuka express no reaction to relentless cursing from her junior Kana. Such was the extent to which Ohana's tail and ears have given her solace.


"What's the matter with you? You've never ever been in a good humour on Mondays..."
"Fu-fu-fu-fu-fu."
"Oh, could it actually be a hopeless _pattern_?"[^1]<!--???-->


Whereas her tail was being touched, Ohana felt a little ticklish and shivered, however Fuyuka paid no heed. For she is an animal lover at heart, she finds  animal parts in Ohana nothing but alluring. And yesterday, when she was given that permission, she truly enjoyed the tail and the ears until she satisfied her craving.


"Haa, I wanna go home~."
"We've only just started... Are you sure you're okay?"


Knowing nothing of circumstances, of course, Kana was no more than puzzled; but she knew that there is no point in pursuing Fuyuka in her flowery condition, and from that point on she been merely giving her a tepid looks.


However, as the time passed and Fuyuka calmed down in a sort, she suddenly flushed up and prostrated herself on the desk.


"I... I'm sorry about that. I have a little something going on."
"I can imagine. I've never seen you with that halfwit face."
"Please, just forget it..."


In front of them, there might be spotted the new _employés_, who had just joined the company. They had finished their outside training, and will be on duty at the head office today.


"Wah—, innocence."


Once upon the time, she used to stand yonder and to have a high hopes for the life to come. At this step, she obliged to believe that it was but preposterous pipe dream.
On the same day of immersing in​ such sentimentality, Fuyuka was ready to leave the office at eight straight. Reasonably early for this firm.


"Thank you for your hard work everyone—."
"Huh, quite fast you are."
"For I've already done what I had to. If you done, why don't we get going?"
"Umm—, well, if you going home, Hiiragi-san, I think I'll go too—."


_Umm—_, Kana stretches and starts readying herself to leave, as Fuyuka does. This company basically has a concept of regular hours, however it is just a mere concept, indeed, and workers staying overtime are as yet plentiful. In despite of the fact that it is not a busy season, somehow there are many who remaining that way, nevertheless she was not blamed for leaving at an early hour.


"New girls were working overtime right away, eh—."
"Working you say, rather reviewing what they've been taught today... How nostalgic."


It has been no different when Fuyuka entered the company as probationer. She would stay at the head office and review what she learned under the guise of overtime work. "It was certainly a necessary thing," believed she, however the question is why be bothered so much as to stay behind.
She, of course, did not say it out loud.


"Well then, see you tomorrow."
"Yes, goodnight—."


After parting with Kana before the office, Fuyuka immediately started on her way  home. Thinking that Ohana is probably awaiting her, while casting her mind back to tail and ears of the girl.

"Flower viewing... is it?"


Upon that very day, the topic occurred out of the blue. The season is spring, it is the peak time for cherry blossom viewing.


"First, do you know what flower viewing is?"
"H-ha~, it is occasion where one occupying oneself in enjoyment of _sakura_ blossoms, is it."
"Quite so, have you ever done it?"
"I have. In my settlement, it used to be a gorgeous annual event. We would prepare tasty meals and drinks and make a lot of noise."


As Fuyuka heard that Ohana was engaged in something similar in her country, she suggested to do something of the sort here, too.
It has been an unsophisticated suggestion, which, however, appeared to please Ohana beyond one's assumptions, and her tail was, _pata-pata_, wriggling to and fro.


"Shall we go on my next day off, then. We'd have to take a train, though."
"That huge carriage. Does the path lies to so distant a place?"
"No, not so distant, but it might be a little complicated with all the transfers and stuff."


After the lunch, Fuyuka employed her time to outline the route they are to take. She should ensure not to get separated as they go, anyhow, however no harm would befall if she unfold an account for it.


"And so we are getting off at this station. Also, although I say flower viewing, I'm the type to walk around instead of parking down and relaxing."
"Eh, you are?"
"Yes, yes. We'd walk down the aisle to view the array of _sakura_ trees."


One of the famous cherry-viewing spots in Tokyo. It should draw in a large audience, obviously, but is to no issue for they solely wish to see cherry blossom. And supposing they are to change the location, too, there are given sites where one may even spread out the sheet. Whilst it is a little risky for the two of them, two females, to be alone at evening, she simply cannot bring herself to invite someone from the company. Hence, they settled down on this format.


"He—, it's like this... Understood! This is so exciting!"
"U-uh-huh."


In despite of Fuyuka's thought "You don't really need to engage so much attention to it", for the girl was helping her make living, was letting her do whatever her fancy was with tail and ears, she felt obliged to show her a gratitude, and to repay, at the least.


"W-well, next holidays then."
"Yes!"


Thinking back, Fuyuka has been so busy that has not gone flower viewing for years. Not until realising it, she herself started to look forward to occasion a little, just a little bit.

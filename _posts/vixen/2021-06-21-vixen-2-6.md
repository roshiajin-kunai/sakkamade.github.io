After finishing the lunch, Fuyuka had taken Ohana to supermarket in front of the station.


"Though we've already been here... today we're here to teach you the money management."


Fuyuka's goal is to teach the girl the value of the money and to hand her some allowance for her daily activities. Fortunately, by slaving in her employment, before she knew she had gathered savings enough, and there was no question in covering these expenses.
<!--excerpt-->

"Fuyuka-san, these money, they so tiny."
"It's normal here. At any rate, there are six kinds of coins and the rest are bills..."


She presented the other with coins and banknotes as she explained. This would  be her first time, as when they came here before Ohana had only selected what she wanted to buy whilst Fuyuka was the one buying it.


"Oh! ah! hee—?"


Whereas walking round the supermarket together with Fuyuka, Ohana was studying the value of money by prices of products. For Fuyuka's part, she considered Ohana was a smart girl, and it appeared to be no mistake as the other kept amassing more and more knowledge.


"Indeed... I believe I have pretty good idea of it now!"


And before long, Ohana effortlessly mastered the Japanese currency. It should not be that difficult, however is different from the currency Ohana previously used.


"Do you really?"


Not that she regarded her as a child, given her age, nevertheless her understanding of it has come far too swiftly. However, for it is unlikely that Ohana would deceive and tell her falsehood, Fuyuka makes a suggestion:


"Then why don't you do the shopping right away?"


From tomorrow, Ohana will be able to go on outings alone. As a prelude (?), Fuyuka determined to let her shop by herself.


"Should that be okay for me?"
"Uh-huh. I'll leave today's dinner up to you, Ohana, so you can buy anything you like."


"Today's..." she said, but, if anything, "Today's as well" is more correct. Since Ohana's arrival, Fuyuka's diet have been utterly taken care of by the girl.


"Understood. May I also decide upon the menu?"
"Yes; but don't cross the contents of my wallet."


There is sufficient to get by as long as she will not buy a lot of pointlessly expensive items, however Fuyuka more or less cautioned her against that.


"Let's see, then, today it shall be..."


After checking the contents of the wallet, Ohana proceed putting groceries into the basket. Being that the girl was so reliant on Fuyuka in practical side of the life​, to be honest, Fuyuka intended to supply her with some advices hereon, sadly, however, making no mistakes, Ohana drew her first shopping trip to a climax quite ordinarily.


"Although I was confused at a moment, I was able to make it safe and sound!"
"You was, eh..."
"Why, what is it?"
"N-no, nothing. So then, shall we go home. I want to spend the reminder of the day relaxing."


They have finished their shopping and now heading home. It was holiday and the streets were crowded. Passing in the midst of a wide range of people from families to couples as they walked, Fuyuka felt indescribable, being accompanied home by Ohana.


(I wonder if anyone among these people may cognise the relation between me and Ohana...)


From the outside, they may be looked upon as at a remote sisters, or possibly even as at a parent and child. At the very least, no one would imagine the girl was her wife.


"What would you do when you home? There is hitherto a time until dinner."
"Um—, some idling, I think. I just want to lie down..."
"Er, are you going to sleep?"
"Mnm, just lie down and loaf."
"H-haa...?"


Ohana appears not to understand much of Fuyuka's vocabulary. Ohana used to living a tightly knit life, and the practice of siesta does not ring a bell.


"You must be tired, too, Ohana. The moment to relax should make you none the worse."


And thusly the girls safely made their way home. After organising purchases, Fuyuka switched on the TV aimlessly. There was an unfamiliar online shopping programme on, for she turned it only for ambient noise, however, she does not mean to watch it.


"Fuwah—, I'm tired."


Before Ohana's eyes there lay Fuyuka upon the futon she laid out earlier. This is typical for a holiday afore Ohana has come.


"U-umm..."
"You can take your time, Ohana. There is the time until dinner."


It was a pathetic statement which implicitly suggests that the dinner was left in the girl's hands, but Fuyuka herself was blind to the fact, probably out of tendency to spend all of her time lounging about.


"E-er—..."


Ohana voiced her distress to the lady keeping lying down all the same. Obviously,  since returning home, she had her concealed ears and tail exposed. There, Fuyuka noted a certain something: Her tail was causing the dress to be severely creased.
It was fine if within the house, but it was not very good visually, for it is rolled up greatly, and she was wearing newly bought undergarment, too. Fuyuka had to look  away slightly, addressing her​.


"By the way, how did you hide the tail and ears? Is that also a part of your spiritual power?"
"Yes, it also is. However, rather than hiding it, I'm merely making it invisible."


When Ohana stated so, but her tail had disappeared. The dress she was wearing, however, was rolled up just as before.


"Merely for it's transparent, like so, doesn't mean it's gone."
"Oh, I see... Eh!? Then it was turned up all the way to the home!?"
"No, I pushed it under my garments at the time... Like, umm, like so."


Ohana folded her tail in a way. Of course, it is visible no longer, and Fuyuka's view reveals but her bottom bulging slightly. It is a little unnatural, but a hundred times better than the dress being rolled up.


"I see. W-well, I guess that's okay. Uh... but when you was wearing a _kimono_ I didn't notice it at all."
"For I was squishing it inside. Such flowy clothing makes it conspicuous."
"Hmm—, I bought it without thinking. I wonder if you will be alright..."
"I shall be alright! You have made this acquirement at such great pains, I shall find a way to cover it!"


Fuyuka felt somewhat uneasy about it. But she could not cut a hole in her clothes for letting the tail through it to keep it from bulging out. If she did that, it would be but perforated one-piece dress from the perspective of others.
With such touch of anxiety, Fuyuka endeavoured to contemplate upon it a little, albeit being unable to do anything about it now, she ultimately resorted to continue lazing about.


The only sound which filled the rooms was high-strung voice of online shopping, and it was kind of like her usual languid holiday. The sort of a holiday where on the whole she sluggishly napping until the nighttime, whereafter naturally gravitating to a nearby supermarket to buy _bento_.


"...What's wrong?"


With sole distinction, however, of Ohana being around. She approached the side of lying Fuyuka, she yawned once, and she flopped down just like that.


"Fuyuka-san, as I watched you, I started to feel sleepy too..."


This morning, Ohana had been turned into a dress-up doll by the clerk at apparel store, and appeared to be worn out from all the walking she had done. Doubtless the fatigue have disclosed itself after making it home. Neither did she seem to have any intention of changing her outfit.


"Fua-ah..."


Unlike her usual sleeping posture, presently, Ohana curled her body up, well as a cat sleeping, and lowered her eyelids.


"For I sleep like this too..."<!--???-->


Not sooner than Ohana closed her eyes, her breath had immediately quieted. As a result, Fuyuka has also been lured into her own sleepiness, and parted with her consciousness.
The only mistake that was made was not setting alarm clock. Not before the sun had completely set, however, had she realised it, hearing the shriek of Ohana, whom she slept with.

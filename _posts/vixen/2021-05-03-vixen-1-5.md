Whilst being anxious of her crimson cheeks due to physical factors, Fuyuka was returning homewards together with vixen named Ohana. Bouncing ears and tail, which had flared out of her body, could not have been left as they were, and upon explaining it the young girl easily made them disappear.


"Since disguise is in our blood! This much is easy as pie!"


Passed from vixen-girl to an ordinary one, Ohana stated so in spirit and followed after Fuyuka. Before one could notice, her attire sustained a change from pure white to pale-coloured _kimono_, and albeit she has not appeared to be the owner of so great a feeling of incongruity, it hitherto is disturbing.


The only bits Fuyuka could learn from conversation on the way to station were that Ohana was cheerful and energetic girl.<!--(potsu-potsu)--> The innocent joyfulness of  child-like speech had slightly brightened her depressed humour; however the facts of not understanding a true character and willingness of the girl to follow her home as matter of course inspired her with reasonable uneasiness.<!--???-->


"I see, so this is the train station where trains run! It's a first time I'm seeing a real thing! Eh, ticket? Do we require this?"


Ohana lacked worldly sense, or rather, situation of modern era had been strange to her. She was excited to see everything from vending machine to automobile, saying she had never seen the real thing  before. "I have to carefully enquire her  of it later," sighs Fuyuka.
The ticket was for Ohana, while she herself used her own transportation smart card. "Oh, what is this!?" she was questioned by Ohana with shining eyes; but since to present succinct explanation have been arduous, it was cut  with "Later."
She has gone through a feeling of an unspeakable guilt, regarding herself somewhat downhearted, but, _Whew_, was relieved  as the girl  began frolicking again at the train's arrival.


"Indeed! So this is a box they call mobile casket, heh."
"Information you have learned seem rather biased, you know..."


Suchwise train departed, reaching the Yogaya, wherein situated apartments inhabited by Fuyuka. This is one of these parts in Tokyo where supermarkets and other facilities are gathered close by the station, at short walk thence lay densely populated residential area.


"Is your abode closely, Hiiragi-sama?"
"It's apartments, about fifteen minutes on foot. Tell me orderly about yourself there, alright?"
"Yes! As your bride, I shall inform you of everything you want to know!"


"I would like to begin from this 'bride' first—," thinking thusly, Fuyuka and Ohana unhurriedly paced their way  through early afternoon of the holiday. Rain have long as stopped, and the sky was crystal clear, cloudless, of sole blue hue.

Fuyuka lives in Tanda Heights, two-story apartment building, and her lodgings had been corner flat on the second floor. The middle rooms are inexpensive, however also are cramped 1K, whilst rent for the corner rooms is a little higher, the layout is larger 1DK,[^1] to slight showing off.
Not too spacious actually, but are decent lodgings for single person to use. The only drawback is that the exterior is pretty shabby, for the building is relatively aged.


"I'm back—..."
"Pardon my intrusion."


A weary voice and a vivacious voice echoed through the air. The room have not been that messy, but was tidy by no means. Once entered into possession—“I'll clean it regularly!"—she was enthusiastic; not-so-used cleaning supplies are forsaken in the nook of the flat.


"Have a seat there for now. I'll bring something to drink."
"Excuse me, thank you very much!"


Escorting Ohana into the room which became the main part of her livelihood, she pours a tea from two-litre plastic bottle into glass.<!--???--> Upon returning, Ohana was wearing her vixen's ears and tail again, and elegantly waiting in neat _seiza_.[^2]
Now and again, however, she let her eyes swim, as though television and small bookshelves about her weightened her mind.


"Alright, here you are."
"Thank you very much!"


Not knowing if Ohana was thirsty, but the girl had her lips on the glass right away. That was hard not to smile at appearance of her drink with utmost effort by her tiny lips. However she cannot allow herself to smile now~.


"Now then."
"Yes?"


Ohana tilts her head at breaking the ice Fuyuka.


"Who are you?"
"Ohana, though."
"Not your name! Ah, where should I start from. Er—..."
"For now, may you let me start from an account of my coming here?"
"Eh? Ah, uh-huh. From this then."


Fuyuka was the only one who raised a voice, Ohana appeared to be considerably at ease. She ought to be under the tension, stepping in to the dwelling of outlander Fuyuka, which expected to be foreign land to her in itself, but there was not a suggestion of such.


"As I briefly mentioned earlier, we are family of _Youko_."[^6]
"Like in kanji for _youkai_ and _kitsune_?"[^3]
"Yes; among the _Youko_ therein also various a families, and we are the family referred as _Kenko_,[^5] who devotes ourselves to serve human."
"_Kenko_..."
"When we reach certain age, we travel to diverse a worlds, wherein we meeting assigned human, and serve them."
"Assigned, human?"
"Yes, of course. Certainly not just by some chance encounter, isn't it right? The person elected under various conditions is given a chance. Indeed... The 'chance,' have there been something like so recently?"
"...Something like so... something, eh—."


Suddenly, she recalled. The leaflet which had been handed to her out of the blue on the way home, when she was saddened. She felt a little eerie over the matter, upon looking back and finding no one there.
Fuyuka got on her feet and brought the leaflet. Somehow she could not bring herself to throw it away, and kept it just in case.


"If _something_, then this flyer—. I called here and they told me to start for that Shinto shrine."
"Ah—, this one, this one!"


Ohana smiles happily, taking the leaflet.


"This actually has a special warding on it. Therefore it should look as mere piece of paper to anyone but you, Hiiragi-sama!"
"Eh? Is that so?"
"Although it is as yet dark for so inexperienced person as myself, the opportunity to meet us in this way is given to those who have been selected based on some sort of criteria."
"Hee...[^4] But, what if I received it and didn't react to it in any way?"
"Such condition would have never arisen. For everything was settled at occasion of handing this."


Fuyuka felt a slight shivers  at such an unquestioning Ohana.<!--???-->

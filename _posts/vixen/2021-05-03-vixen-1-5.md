While under an anxiety about her crimson cheeks due to physical factors, Fuyuka was returning homewards together with vixen named Ohana. Bouncing ears and tail, which flared out of her body, could not be left as they were, and explaining it, the young girl readily made them vanish.


"For disguise is in our blood! This much is easy as pie!"


Passed from vixen-girl to an ordinary one, Ohana stated so in spirit and followed after Fuyuka. Before one could notice, her attire sustained a change from pure white to pale-coloured _kimono_, and albeit she has not appeared to be the owner of so great a feeling of incongruity, it hitherto is disturbing.


The only bits Fuyuka learned from conversation on the way to the station were that Ohana was cheerful and energetic girl.<!--(potsu-potsu)--> Innocent joyfulness of  child-like speech slightly brightened her depressed humour; however the facts of not understanding a true character and willingness of the girl to follow her home as matter of course inspired her with noticeable uneasiness.<!--???-->


"I see, this is, then, the train​ station, where trains run! It's my first time seeing a real one! Eh, ticket? Do we require it?"


Ohana has been world away, or rather, situation of modern era was stranger to her. She was excited to view everything from vending machine to automobile, saying she has never seen the real one before. "I should carefully enquire her about it later," sighs Fuyuka.
The ticket was for Ohana, whilst she herself employed her own transportation smart card. "Oh, what is this!?" she was questioned by Ohana with shining eyes; but offer a succinct explanation would be arduous, and it was cut short with "Later."
Experience through a feeling of an unspeakable guilt, regarding the other somewhat downhearted, _Whew_, she was relieved  as the girl  began frolicking once again at the train's arrival.


"Indeed! So this is a box they call mobile casket, heh."
"You know, a knowledge you acquired seem rather biased..."


Suchwise train departed, reaching the Yogaya, wherein situated apartments inhabited by Fuyuka. This is one of these parts in Tokyo where supermarkets and other facilities are gathered nigh by the station, at short walk thence lay densely populated residential area.


"Is your abode close, Hiiragi-sama?"
"It's apartment, about fifteen minutes on foot. Tell me orderly about yourself there, alright?"
"Yes! As your bride I shall inform you of everything you want to learn!"


"I would like to begin from this 'bride' first—," thinking thusly, Fuyuka and Ohana unhurriedly paced their way  through early afternoon of the holiday. Rain has long as stopped, and the sky was crystal clear, cloudless, of sole azure hue.

Fuyuka lives in Tanda Heights, two-story apartment building, and her lodgings is a corner flat on the second floor. The middle rooms are less expensive, however also are cramped 1K, whilst rent for the corner rooms is a little higher, the layout is larger 1DK,[^1] to minor showing-off.
Not too spacious, in truth, but are decent lodgings for single person to settle in. The only drawback is that the exterior is pretty shabby, for building is relatively aged.


"I'm back—..."
"Pardon my intrusion!"


A weary voice and a vivacious voice echoed through the air. The room have not been that messy, but was tidy by no means. Once entered into possession—“I'll clean regularly!"—she was enthusiastic; not-so-used supplies for cleaning chore now are forsaken in the nook of the flat.


"You can have a seat here. I'll bring something to drink."
"Excuse me, thank you very much!"


Escorting Ohana into the room which became the main part of her livelihood, she pours a tea from two-litre plastic bottle into the glass.<!--???--> Returning, Ohana had her vixen's ears and tail again, and elegantly awaited in neat _seiza_.[^2]
Although, now and again, she let her eyes swim, as if television and small bookshelves about her weighted her mind.


"Alright, here you are."
"Thank you very much!"


Fuyuka was ignorant whether Ohana was thirsty, but the girl had her lips on the glass right away. It was hard not to smile at appearance of her drink with an utmost effort by her tiny lips. However she cannot admit a smile, now—.


"So then."
"Yes?"


Ohana tilt her head at breaking the ice Fuyuka.


"Who are you?"
"Ohana, though."
"Not your name! Ah, where should I start from. Er—..."
"For now, may you let me start from an account of my coming here?"
"Eh? Ah, uh-huh. From this then."


Fuyuka was the only one who raised a voice, Ohana appeared to be considerably at ease. Albeit she ought to be under the tension, stepping in to the dwelling of outlander, Fuyuka, which expected to be foreign land to her in itself, there was not a suggestion to such.


"As I briefly mentioned earlier, we are family of _Youko_."[^6]
"Like in kanji for _youkai_ and _kitsune_?"[^3]
"Yes; among the _Youko_ therein also various a families, and we are the family referred as _Kenko_,[^5] who devotes ourselves to serve human."
"_Kenko_..."
"As we reach certain age we travel to diverse a worlds, wherein we meeting assigned human, and serve them."
"Assigned... human?"
"Yes, of course. Certainly not someone by mere chance encounter, isn't it right? The person elected under various conditions is given a chance. Indeed... The 'chance', have there been something like so recently?"
"...Something like so, huh—. Something..."


Suddenly, she recalled the leaflet handed to her out of the blue on the way home, as she was saddened. She felt a little eerie over the matter, looking back and finding no one there.
Fuyuka got on her feet and produced the leaflet. Somehow, she was unable to bring herself to throw it away, and kept it just in case.


"If _something_, then this flyer—. I called here and they assigned me an appointment at that Shinto shrine."
"Ah—, this one, this one!"


Ohana smiles happily, taking the leaflet.


"This actually has a special warding on it. Therefore, it should look as nothing but piece of paper to anyone but you, Hiiragi-sama!"
"Eh? It is?"
"Although it is as yet dark for so inexperienced person as myself, the opportunity to meet us in this way is given to those who have passed selection based on some sort of criteria."
"Hee...?[^4] But, what if I received it and didn't react in any way?"
"Such circumstances would have never arisen. For everything was settled at occasion of handing this."


Fuyuka felt a slight shivers  at such an unquestioning Ohana.<!--???-->

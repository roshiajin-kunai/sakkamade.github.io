On the following holiday, Fuyuka accompanied Ohana to determined place<!--flower viewing-->. Ohana was clothed in attire they had just bought the other day, and her back was a little puffy, likely for her transparent tail which was packed within.


(Well, no one will notice this much. It's too crowded...)


The hour has been past seven in the evening and night was upon them. They came at this hour for cherry blossoms are pretty when illuminated. If Ohana would find this sight fresh was more than Fuyuka could tell, however the girl's eyes were fixed upwards since a while ago.


"Wonderful! It's wonderful! Flowers in my parts used to be lit by bonfires, at night, but here it is so much brighter!"
"Bonfires might also have their charm, though I like it here, too."


Fuyuka was curiously well-versed as she watched admiring Ohana, even though she had not been here for a few years due to either work fatigue or regarding it too much a bother.
And so they walked along the path lined with cherry trees, carrying drinks they bought beforehand at a nearby convenience store.
Of course, for it has been very famous spot for flower viewing, an extraordinary congestion there was. None the less, it appears that some basic etiquette is being observed there still, as everyone proceed slowly, at regular intervals.


"Try not to get separated. There are so many people."


Fuyuka said and opened the can of beer which she bought. She is not a heavy drinker, but it is not uncommon for her to drink at places like this.
Ohana, meanwhile, had a juice; although she said to be fine with alcohol, too. None the less, regardless of her actual age, in no wise could she afford it, for she has been a little girl in both appearance and character.


"Eep, w-wait..."


Ohana had nodded in response to​ Fuyuka's reminder, but was soon swept away  by overwhelming crowd all the same. Not because she is in fault, but because all round her are adults, and everyone is focused on cherry blossoms, naturally Ohana is quite out of the sight.


"Hold on, are you good!?"


Fuyuka rushed to reach for Ohana, who swayed unsteadily under the pressure. Ohana, too, finding this unbearable, grasped the outstretched hand and regained her bearings somehow.


"E-excuse me. I am apologise to cause you inconvenience..."
"No problem. Everybody seems to be enthralled by cherry trees, there is nothing we can do."


Fuyuka glanced at Ohana while going along with the boisterous, chattering mass. The girl appeared to be admiring the cherry blossoms, not paying undue attention to their hand-holding.


(Why am I so conscious of this? We are not in primary school...!)


Ohana's hand is tender and warm. That warmth naturally helped to keep Fuyuka's hand warmer as, even in spring, the evenings are still chilly.


(It is the only way to prevent us getting separated, it can't be helped... And Ohana doesn't seem to be concerned about it either, I suppose I should keep it normal...)


She sipped the beer in her other hand. The power of alcohol is much welcomed, presently.


"Ohana, um..."
"Yes?"
"Do you, do you enjoy outings like this?"
"Yes! I love it!"
"You do. I'm happy to hear that. I wanted to apologise that you always taking care of everything: cooking, cleaning."

Due to about them, she does not reproach Ohana for her remark. After all, it is impossible for someone to perceive their relationship within this environment.
Smilingly, Ohana continued:


"Withal, for you are so caring, Fuyuka-san! I'm truly loving it, too!"
"Caring? Me?"
"Yes! So much as providing with your lodgings this unlooked-for visitor, and even, thus, bringing me along here, to the flower viewing!"
"That's just..."


The way of showing appreciation for everything she does for her sake.<!-- changed --> On weekdays, she takes care of her dwelling, and although the place is small, she cleans it every day, so the rooms are cleaner than ever, as well as meals which watching over Fuyuka's health, for they are not side dishes from supermarket, but prepared so earnestly.
This merely assumed to be a thank you for that...


(What is this with me!)<!--???-->


Fuyuka writhed in an unfamiliar emotion. She believed she was past the idea of a love affair, after what happened; however, somewhere in the depths of her heart, she was slowly being drawn to Ohana.
Not willing to acknowledge the fact, Fuyuka, _bun-bun_,[^1] shook her head violently, dismissing a wicked thought.

Indeed, in the face of holiday, Kana has been dressed in her work suit. Reply escorted a merry smile.


"Nah~, I had some work to do today—... More work is the best rest,[^2] they say, eh—. And there was that new girl, and I told her about this place, and she said she wanted to go, and so..."
"Eh? New girl?"


Upon such statement, when she looked behind Kana, there stay not less than three, still not much wrinkled, suited youthful figures. Not before they noted Fuyuka, they bowed with "Thank you for the hard work!"


"Y-yeah... Are you on holiday yet?"
"'You'd learn everything much faster if you'll study at work,' were director's reason."
"......"


Fuyuka frowned, reminded of the snide face of her director. She regarded, no matter how you look at it, cruel it is to force them work on holidays as soon as they finished internship.
Feeling sorry for poor newcomers, Kana spotted the hand which linked together Fuyuka with little girl: Oh dear, she spotted it.


"Incidentally, Hiiragi-san! who is this girl holding your hand!?"

"Oh dear," Fuyuka's thought went blank. She had all but forgotten that she was now here flower viewing with Ohana.


"Hm-m, um—... she is..."


Struggling to cunningly excuse herself, _brush_, Ohana briskly stepped forwards, and swiftly bower her head.

When Ohana politely announced this, Kana's eyes lit up.


"Kya—! What a cutie—! Ohana-chan, are you! I'm Kana, Hiiragi-san is always taking care of me at work!"


Ohana skilfully listen and nod to the words of intoxicated Kana. The responses were perfectly serene and nonchalant.


"So Hiiragi-san came with Ohana-chan to flower viewing—. You can't just suddenly come with your relatives and people from company, right."
"R-right, that's right. Which is why I'm sorry."
"No, no, it's good—. Even so, Ohana-chan is so small and cute~. _Yosh~yosh_!"
"Hh!"


From Kana's point, she witness her but as a child, therefore, she began stroking the hair of Ohana without reservation.


"Fwah... Ah!?"


For Ohana likewise that was past expectations, and she let out a cry of surprise, it was all too late, however. When Ohana found her head dishevelled, Kana paused.


"Um? Umm?"


There are, of course, the ears of Ohana which are but transparent, and one can feel them if one touch them. The moment Kana aimed to move her hand farther, to ascertain——


"Stop it!"


Reflexively, Fuyuka covered Ohana, pulling her into her arms.


"Whoa!?"<!--???-->


Even inebriated Kana was taken aback by this, and retreated a little. Fuyuka hastily open her lips after shielding the other.


"Oh-Ohana is polite but she's timid person, if you touch her all of a sudden, she'll get startled!"
"A-ah, right. I'm sorry, I'm a bit tipsy... Ohana-chan, too, I'm sorry for such rude attack, okay?"
"No, I has been at a slight dismay, but... I am alright."


Kana felt saved by smiling Ohana saying so while being wrapped in the arms of Fuyuka from behind.


"Excuse me, I guess I was excited to see Hiiragi-san on our day off. Well, then, we'd better get going."
"Oh, indeed. What are you planning to do now?"
"Eh? We all agreed to go drinking, aren't we! What are you talking about!"
"You're still going to drink... Try not to ruffle too many feathers."[^3]
"Aye—! _Yosh_, then, let's go, people—!"


New _employés_ were cordial in their manners as they bid farewell to Fuyuka and Ohana, and followed behind Kana, as yet leaving an impression of reticence and innocence: how nostalgic.


"Um, Fuyuka-san."


As she watched them go she heard a voice coming from Ohana. Right then she realised that she has hitherto been embracing her thereat; she panicked and freed the girl.


"Oh, s-sorry!"
"No, no, you protected me, have you not? I shall apologise as well. I endeavoured to delude them, and yet..."
"It's fine. I must say you did pretty well... Did you think it over in advance?"
"Yes! I thought of this for no one to come to find my being the wife of Fuyuka-san's."
"You thought of this..."


Ordinarily, she ought to be delighted about it. For Fuyuka it meant one less problem to worry of.
However, the moment Ohana had spoken a falsehood of her person being a relative, the moment Kana patted her head... certainly, at that time, Fuyuka's heart confronted the loneliness, jealousy and possessiveness.


"Say, Ohana."
"Yes?"


She squeezed her hand slightly tighter than earlier.


"Why don't we eat _sushi_? They also have _inarizushi_ there."
"Is this true!? I'd love to!"


Her not-visible ears and tail seemed to be moving; with a small smile upon her visage, Fuyuka busied herself with sorting out the emotion which had sprouted within.

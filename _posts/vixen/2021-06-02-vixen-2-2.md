Fuyuka could not help being afeard for Ohana, but pitiless society take it in no account.
Leisurely working as usual, by noon she bought a decent bun and a coffee at  convenience store for lunch. Thence, she keeps working until the evening, and lingers over her work until nine, snacking on the confections.


"Good day, everyone—."


And finally, exchanging a greetings with colleagues, she departed from her workplace. On the whole, all the passings are  pretty much the same as always.


"See you, Hiiragi-san—."


But not sooner than Fuyuka parted with Kana, who was similarly on overtime too, from thence her step has been different from the groove.


"Oh, Hiiragi-san...?"


Kana stared suspiciously at so distant a person of her senior, who has ever been trudging homewards, burdened by dark aura upon the shoulders.

<br>


However swift her stride is, she would still be unable to buy hours back, and at most, only dozen of minutes.
And yet, she was anxious for Ohana. If anything, rather than for Ohana herself, for leaving the stranger at home while setting off to work.


(Although I told her she can eat, take a bath, and go to bed first...)


She expects to return before ten. However, even by virtue of their brief association, she gathered that Ohana will be waiting without sleep.


"Sure enough..."


Following Fuyuka's insight, when finally reaching apartment, her chambers were lit. That was quite anticipated, so she did not panic, and producing her key she opened the door.


"Ah, welcome home!"
"I'm home... Were you waiting for me? You could just go to sleep."


Ohana briskly ran to returned Fuyuka. For it was so long since someone greeted her at home, she was a little, just a tiniest iota, excited.


"No, no; for this is my duty, Fuyuka-san, to welcome you after laborious day! Which do you prefer: the bath, the dinner?"


"'Or perhaps, me?' as follow up, eh," popped up a minute thought in Fuyuka; but she shook off her evil passions, wondering what kind of middle-aged adult she is. Pure and innocent Ohana will never say that, in the first place.


"I'd love to take a bath, but... it will take time to make ready, right?"
"No, it heated up just now! You may go in at once!"<!--???-->
"Eh...? But I said I don't know when I'm back, because of late overtimes."
"Well, you may call it sixth sense... I am not sure, but I may usually foresee it."
"Because you are _Youko_?"
"Hmmm—, because of that—?"


"Why; you don't know that?" was about to sharply question Fuyuka; but she chose the bath while she has an opportunity. On weekdays, she has not much time, and  takes shower commonly, her true heart, however, lies to soak herself within a hot water, if possible.


"Nice hot bath..."


Enjoyed the bath presumed upon such great kindness of Ohana, Fuyuka came out   to find the meals for two arranged upon the dining table.


"By no means, Ohana... Dinner was also waiting!?"
"Yes; I have been waiting for you, though? The evening is late now, I made a small, easy-to-digest menu."


As Ohana say, on the table there stay moderate portions of meal, mainly _Tamagotoji udon_,[^1] which seemed to be easy on stomach.


"Th, thank you. I'm happy to hear it, but were you hungry? It's so much unlike the life you had, Ohana."


The time was about ten. Yesterday she was already asleep by now. It should be hard on Ohana who has a custom of going to bed early. Whereas Fuyuka been ill at ease, girl replies, smilingly.


"Surely to say I'm not sleepy would be false, however to eat together like this is more delicious, is not it. And also, I feel lonely eating by myself."
"...Umm—, if you say so... alright then."


Being told that, Fuyuka was incapable of potent denial, and the food was getting colder after all, so she joined her palms together.


"Fuyuka-san, what kind of work are you doing?"
"Umm—, looking the monitor... not sure... Slothfully tapping the keyboard, I think—."
"???"


To eat while chatting with someone. This have become commonplace since Ohana came, but it certainly tastes better than eating _bento_ alone... Or so she feel.


"That was tasty!"[^2]
"Thank you for the meal. You sure can  cook anything, Ohana, are you?"
"I've more or less studied Japanese, Western and Chinese cousin, however I'm not so confident in Chinese cooking yet. Although I fancy to give it a try at some point!"
"I... see... Thank you very much, that was delicious. I'm sorry about everything."
"No, no, it makes me happy to hear you say that!"


Stating so, Ohana was about to start washing the dishes, which were naturally deprived by Fuyuka. However many ears and tails she has, she was still a child on the outside, and it pained the conscience so to let her go that far.
Fuyuka directed Ohana to  take a bath after resting for awhile, and started dish-washing.


"Ara...?"[^3]


Thereat, too, was waiting her one more realisation.


"I don't remember it being this beautiful..."


The kitchen, the area about the gas stove, looked excessively beautiful. No, truly beautiful. Minor spots all over the kitchen, oil stains upon the gas stove gone leaving no trace behind.


(Come to think of it, the air in the whole flat appears refreshing...)


If one were to peek into the space between the refrigerator and the wall, they find no accumulated blackish dust.
Fuyuka hurried to the bathroom, which she had entered a short while ago.


"And here as well..."


No water blots typical of unit-baths. Doubtless it was Ohana who did it. She was the only one who could.


"Eh, what is the matter?"


As  Fuyuka was peeping in to such bathroom, a voice called out from behind her. There was Ohana holing only a towel to wipe her body down.


"Oh, Ohana... Say, have you did some cleaning today by any chance?"
"Yes? Let me see, I had nothing particularly to do and plenty of cleaning supplies, so I did it. Per, perchance I shouldn't have!?"


The cleaning supplies were bought for systematic cleaning chore, right before were abandoned. She has been a quintessential sloth.


"No... rather than shouldn't... Please, Ohana-sama, forgive me; kindly take your time..."<!--???-->
"Eh, w, what? Fuyuka-san???"


Escorting Ohana of the countenance that as though exclaimed "What are you saying!?" to the bathroom, Fuyuka sighed out.


"Well, if you deeply-deeply think about it, cleaning  may be the only thing she had to do, even so to let her do everything..."


Fuyuka was despondent, pasting upon herself a label of hopeless being. She was ashamed of doing so little as dish-washing earlier and thinking she doing her bit.<!--???-->


"A, anyway, we can do it from now on! Uh-huh, right!"<!--???-->


Fuyuka was unsure wether she can do it, but making up her mind she ended up with nothing but washing the dishes this time.
Then, a little while later, Ohana appeared from the bathroom. She was already dressed in what looked like a sleepwear, Fuyuka had no idea if that was the part of the _Youko_s' power. Albeit, the only thing she found slightly eccentric was the tail that was enwrapped in towel.


"This reminds me, why have you wrapped the towel around—and yesterday too?"
"Eh? I'm doing this for I have to dry the tail. Only the tail is slow to dry."


She tightly wrung it out, then enveloped it in a towel, and waiting for it to dry.<!--???--> Yesterday and the day before yesterday, Fuyuka been watchfully waiting and wondering what was it for; it was obvious after nicely taking a thought.


"But, why not just use a hair-dryer?"
"_Hairdraier_?"
"...Come here."


"I  have to make sure how deep her knowledge about home appliances," thought Fuyuka.


"Fuwaah—."


Having Ohana sit on top of the futon, the current of warm air from the hair-dryer was directed to her tail, while it was being combed. The more Ohana's tail dried, the more it became fluffy and silky, it was a blissful moment for Fuyuka, who was able to touch it in a natural flow.


(We should make it a habit from now on.)


Ohana moaned pleasantly when her tail, which was getting bushier and bushier, was gently brushed.


"Come to think of it... is it okay to touch the tail like this?"


In the world of fiction, this is a delicate region, however if one were to look at so meekly patted Ohana, it does not seem that way.


"It's a little ticklish, I shall say—. Ah, however the base is rather sensitive, I get startled if someone suddenly touch it."
"Oh, I see."


Thence, the time streamed in silence. Fuyuka was  able to enjoy the mild tail unexpectedly, she was touching it freed from obstructive thought​. The warm, downy fur seemed to blow away her daily fatigue by mere touch, twice as much in case of animal lover.


"_Yosh_, this is it. Ohana?"


Fuyuka turned off the hair-dryer as the tail was completely dry. And then spoke to her; but there was no reply.


"Ohana...?"


Calling her once again, Ohana, _slump_, lost her support and lay down upon the futon.


"Wait... W, what's wrong?"
"_Zzz, zzz_..."


For how long Ohana has been asleep was left unknown, but her breathing was regular. Drying her hair upon the futon served her well.


"You was sleepy after all."


As she touched the tip of her ear with fingertip, _pokun_, it  jerked promptly. Covering her so that not to wake her up, Fuyuka set her alarm clock and lay down as well.


"Good night."


Sensing quiet breath from the side, she likewise embarked for the world of the dream.

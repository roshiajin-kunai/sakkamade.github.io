Fuyuka could not help being afeard for Ohana, but pitiless society take it in no account.
Leisurely working as usual, by noon she bought a decent bun and a coffee at  convenience store for lunch. Whereafter she kept working until the evening, and lingered over her work until nine, snacking on the confections.


"Good day, everyone—."


And finally, exchanging greetings with colleagues, she departed from her workplace. On the whole, all the passings are  pretty much the same as ever.


"See you, Hiiragi-san—."


But not sooner than Fuyuka parted with Kana, who was similarly on overtime too, thence her step has been much different from the groove.


"Oh, Hiiragi-san...?"


Kana stared suspiciously at so distant from her senior a person, who has always been trudging homewards, burdened by dark aura upon the shoulders.

<br>


However swift her stride is, she would still be unable to buy hours back, only dozen of minutes at most.
And yet, she was anxious for Ohana. If anything, rather than for Ohana herself, for leaving stranger at home while setting off to work.


(Although I told her she can eat, take a bath, and go to bed first...)


She expects to return before ten. However, even by virtue of their brief association, she could gather that Ohana will be waiting without sleep.


"Sure enough..."


Following Fuyuka's insight, when finally reaching apartment, her chambers were still lit. That was quite anticipated and she did not panic; producing her key she opened the door.


"Ah, welcome home!"
"I'm home... Were you waiting for me? You could just go sleep."


Ohana briskly ran to returned Fuyuka. It has been so long since someone greeted her at home, so she was a little, just a tiniest iota, excited.


"No, no; for this is my duty, Fuyuka-san, to welcome you after laborious day! Which do you prefer: the bath, the dinner?"


"'Or perhaps, me?' as follow up?" for a moment contemplated Fuyuka; though she shook her evil passions off, "How have I become this kind of middle-aged weirdo." Pure and innocent Ohana will never say that, in the first place.


"I'd love to take a bath, but... it will take a time to make it ready, right?"
"No, it has heated up just now! You may go in at once!"<!--???-->
"Eh...? But I said I don't know when I'm back because of late overtimes."
"Well, you may call it sixth sense... I am not sure, but I may usually foresee it."
"Because you are _Youko_?"
"Hmmm—, because of that—?"


"Why; you don't know that?" was about to sharply question Fuyuka, but she chose the bath while she has an opportunity. On weekdays, she do not have much time, and  takes a shower commonly. If possible, however, her true heart lies to soak herself in a hot water.


"Nice hot bath..."


Enjoyed the bath presumed upon such great kindness of Ohana, Fuyuka came out   to find the meals for two laid out upon the dining table.


"By no means, Ohana... Dinner was also waiting!?"
"Yes; I have been waiting for you, though? The evening is late now, I made a small, easy-to-digest menu."


As Ohana say, on the table there lay moderate portions of meals, with _Tamagotoji udon_[^1] as main, which seemed to be easy on stomach.


"Th, thank you. I'm happy to hear it, but were you hungry? It's so much unlike the life you had, Ohana."


The time was about ten. Yesterday she was already asleep by now. It should be hard on Ohana and her custom of going to bed early. Whereas Fuyuka been ill at ease, girl replies, smilingly:


"Surely to say I'm not sleepy would be false, however to eat together with you like this, it is more delicious, is not it. And also, I feel lonely eating by myself."
"...Umm—, if you are putting it like this... Alright then, I guess."


Fuyuka was incapable of potent denial, and the food was getting colder, after all, therefore she joined her palms together.


"What kind of occupation do you have, Fuyuka-san?"
"Umm—, looking the monitor... Not sure... Slothfully tapping the keyboard, I think—."
"???"


To eat while chatting with someone. This have become natural since Ohana's arrive, and it certainly tastes better than eating _bento_ alone... Or so she feel.


"That was tasty!"[^2]
"Thank you for the meal. You sure can  cook anything, eh?"
"I've more or less studied Japanese, Western and Chinese cousin, however I'm not so confident in Chinese cooking yet. Although I fancy to give it a try at some point!"
"I... see... Thank you very much, that was delicious. I'm sorry about everything."
"No, no, it makes me happy to hear you say that!"<!--???-->


Stating so, Ohana was about to start washing the dishes, but they were naturally deprived by Fuyuka. Whichever ears and tail she has, she was still a child on the outside, and it pained the conscience so to let her go that far.
Fuyuka directed Ohana to  take a bath after resting for awhile, and started dish-washing.


"_Ara_...?"[^3]


Thereat, too, was waiting for her one more realisation.


"I don't remember it being this beautiful..."


The kitchen, the area about the gas stove, looked excessively beautiful. No, truly beautiful. Minor spots all over the kitchen, oil stains upon the gas stove have gone leaving no trace behind.


(Come to think of it, the air in the whole flat appears refreshing...)


If one were to peek into the space between the refrigerator and the wall, they would find no accumulated blackish dust even there.
Fuyuka hastened to the bathroom, which she used a short while ago.


"And here as well..."


No water blots typical of unit-baths. Doubtless it was Ohana's doing. She and only she is the one who could.


"Eh, what is the matter?"


As  Fuyuka was peeping in to such bathroom, a voice called out from behind her. There was Ohana holing nothing but a towel for wiping-her-body-business.<!--???-->


"Oh, Ohana... Say, did you make some cleaning today by any chance?"
"Yes? Let me see, I did it for I had nothing particularly to do and plenty of supplies for cleaning chore. Per, perchance I shouldn't have!?"


Cleaning supplies were bought for systematic cleaning chore, right before were forsaken. She has been a quintessential sloth.


"No... rather than shouldn't... Please, Ohana-sama, forgive me; kindly take your time..."<!--???-->
"Eh, w, what? Fuyuka-san???"


Escorting Ohana of the countenance that as though exclaimed "Why, I don't understand!?" to the bathroom, Fuyuka sighed out.


"Well, if you think about it, deeply-deeply, the cleaning perhaps was the only thing she had to do, even so to let her do everything..."


Fuyuka was despondent, pasting upon herself a label of hopeless being. She was ashamed of doing so little as dish-washing earlier and thinking she is doing her bit.<!--???-->


"A, anyway, we can do it from now on! Uh-huh, right!"<!--???-->


Fuyuka was unsure wether she actually can, but making up her mind she ended with nothing but washing the dishes this time.
Then, a little while later, Ohana appeared from the bath. She was already dressed in what looked like sleepwear, Fuyuka not known if that was the part of the _Youko's_ power. Albeit, one thing she found slightly eccentric, it was the tail which was bundled up in a towel.


"This reminds me, why are you wrapping it around—and yesterday too?"
"Eh? I'm doing this for I have to dry the tail. Only the tail is slow to dry."


She tightly wrung it out, then enveloped it in a towel, and now waiting for it to dry.<!--???--> Yesterday and the day before yesterday, Fuyuka been watchfully waiting and wondering what was it for; though it was obvious after nicely taking a thought.


"But, why not just use a hair-dryer?"
"_Hairdraier_?"
"...Come here."


"I have to make sure how profound her knowledge about home appliances as well," thought Fuyuka.


"Fuwaah—."


As Ohana sit upon the futon the current of warm air from hair-dryer was directed to her tail, which was being combed withal. The more Ohana's tail dried up, the more it became fluffy and silky, it was blissful moment for Fuyuka, who was able to touch it in a natural flow.


(We should make it a habit from now on.)


Ohana moaned pleasantly when her tail was gently brushed, getting bushier and bushier.


"Come to think of it... is it okay to touch the tail like this?"


In the world of fiction, this is a delicate region, if one were to look at so meekly patted Ohana, however, it does not seem to be that way.


"It's a little ticklish, I shall say—. Ah, but the base is rather sensitive, I would get startled if someone suddenly touch it."
"Oh, I see."


And then, the time streamed in silence. Unexpectedly, Fuyuka was  able to enjoy the mild tail, and was touching it freed from an obstructive thought. The warm, downy fur seemed to blow away her daily fatigue by mere touch, twice as much in case of animal lover.


"_Yosh_, this is it. Ohana?"


Fuyuka turned off the hair-dryer upon accomplishing the job, and spoke to her; but there was no reply.


"Ohana...?"


Calling her once again, Ohana, _slump_, lost her support and flopped down upon the futon.


"Wait... W, what's wrong?"
"_Zzz, zzz_..."


For how long Ohana has been asleep was left obscure, but her breathing was regular so far. Drying her hair upon the futon served her well.


"You was sleepy after all."


As she touched the tip of her ear with fingertip, _pokun_, it  jerked promptly. Covering her carefully, so that not to wake her up, Fuyuka set her alarm clock and lay down alongside.


"Good night."


Sensing quiet breath from the nearby, she had also left for the world of the dream.

"Uunh——..."


Sighed Fuyuka for umpteenth time today at the groan and the suspicious gaze of her junior Kana.


"Like I said, it's nothing."
"Nay—! It's definitely something! After all, it's weird, it's Wednesday and I  still haven't seen these dead eyes of yours!!"[^1]
"So plainly rude you are..."
<!--excerpt-->

It is the first week as Ohana arrived. Today is Wednesday, which also marks the half-way point.
As a matter, of course, she was late to return on both Monday and Tuesday, and although motivation has not been something she had ever had, in the first place, mysteriously, Fuyuka feel neither too languid nor too tired.


"Where's Hiiragi-san who always seems dying on Wednesdays and Thursdays, and is just teensy bit better on Fridays before holidays?"
"Well, I don't know... It's fine, even I can be cheerful once in a while."


Kana, plainly seeing through such facade of Fuyuka, was involved with her since the early morning. Incidentally, Fuyuka's impression of her is that she  perpetually in relatively good spirits. How enviable.


"It's fine for you to be cheerful... But! That's not the question!"


Stating this much, Kana lowered​ her voice slightly, and murmured, grinning:


"...Could it be some boyfriend, perhaps—?"


For an instant, Fuyuka nigh gasped. She does not have "some boyfriend", but bride who bypassed the formalities; however, the only factor in her slightly unusual vitality must be this very bride.


"I, I have no boyfriend or anything. But... Indeed, I just tried to keep early hours and healthy diet."
"Eh—, really—? You are quite popular, Hiiragi-san, I was hoping perhaps you having some in-house romance..."


Kana hung her head, downcast.


"What are you expecting and disappointing about? Or rather, what do you mean 'popular'?"
"I mean exactly this."


In fact, the words of Kana was not a misstatement.
The person herself has no intention of being such at all, but she work silently day-in day-out without a word of objection, not berating each and every one as her supervisor does, still appearing to be peaceful all together, and in spite of all this she is fairly good-looking.
Again, even if the person herself does not whatever mean to shape herself that way, surroundings cannot but recognise her as such.


"Look, don't say silly things, I should get to work. I must be home as early as possible."
"...As early as possible... Perhaps because someone is waiting for you there?"
"......Because I won't be able to eat otherwise."


The dubiousness ever more shaded the pupils of Kana's eyes, Fuyuka, however, was able to somehow cut her way through the situation (?) by exercising her right to silence.


◆


"Welcome home!"


She succeed to put in an appearance back home barely afore ten o'clock. Today, however, upon the grounds of visiting the supermarket to pick up some ingredients for meal.


"Wah, pray accept my apologies—apologies for making you acquire all this."
"It's totally fine. It is me who locked you up, Ohana... Just two more days, okay?"


They scheduled to start off shopping on Saturday, planned also to enlighten Ohana the money management, and to obtain the clothes for her going-out-alone-business.
So to speak, unto then, Ohana has been as though situated under house arrest; Fuyuka felt awful for that.


"No need to worry! I am yet to dust the lodgings, and I had the experience, too, of watching this _Tivi_!"
"Oh, I see... And what was on TV?"
"Today, there was something called 'Mu-vi' at noon, and so I was watching it. It was of a bunch of sharks."


"I wonder if this is something to panic about," concluded Fuyuka, slightly  relieved as the other appeared to bear her liberty in her own way.
Fuyuka has hitherto not completely welcomed Ohana into her life, although being good-natured as she is, she cannot chase the girl out, and along with so hectic an everyday life which she has, she kept the girl at her rooms under excuse of "Oh well, there is nothing I can do."


(Obviously she herself wouldn't wish to remain for loo long...)


The idea desperately adhered to the depth of Fuyuka's mind. Being cast aside by her last girlfriend, she subconsciously suffer a trauma.


"Come, take your bath kindly. I shall prepare the meal!"
"Th, thank you."


However sadly it is, in mere few days, Fuyuka had her livelihood presently tinted  by Ohana.


(I can't help it, can I! Getting home and here this hot bath ready and these tasty meals prepared and this precious girl smiling!)


Both vixen's ears and tail were solely complements, from Fuyuka's point.
Recently, for she began to use the dryer upon Ohana's hair after her bath at the occasion she may pat Ohana to her heart's content, and furthermore, for Ohana usually dozing off in the middle, she may stroke her however her heart desire for certain while.
Albeit each and every time Fuyuka fails to shoo away gradually overcoming somnolence while feeling the touch of her tail.


In such fashion first week with Ohana had consumed itself. As ever Fuyuka was languid at work; but as a result of Ohana's assistance in domestic chores, she felt as though she was moving forth.


And finally, the Saturday comes.


"Then, shall we."
"Yes! Kindly!"


The Saturday of lesson for Ohana  upon the life under this world has commenced.

In the first place, even after being told of their usage of something as fixed regulations, Fuyuka could not quite follow.
At any rate, the most worrisome point: What are intentions of Ohana before her eyes from here on.


"As such, since you're bride, we will be living together, isn't this so?"
"So, so that is, of course...!"


Not a trace of fluctuate was noted within an answer of Ohana to this question. She indeed intends to reside together with Fuyuka in these apartments.
None the less cohabitation with girl of appearance of tender years, even of the same sex, is not something which might lightly be nodded to.


(How does she plan to obtain a resident card and go through whole lot of other formalities, to begin with! Or rather, what happens if someone finds out that I brought a child of this apparent age to my house!? Will I be arrested!? I will be arrested!?)


Fuyuka turns pale, picturing her taking into custody on suspicion of abduction. She does not long to be taken away wearing a mask, and receiving shutters from the mass media.


(No-, no good, I have to refuse.)


Fuyuka thought so once again as she brought the girl to the house. Ohana before her eyes gazed at lady of such mental agony with a look of puzzlement.


"Excuse me, is something troubling you? I shall do whatever I can!"
"No, I just still don't have enough information to know if it's a trouble."
"Information? Pray ask me anything you want to! We are bride-bride couple from now on! Let us avoid as many secrets as possible!"


Accent of the expression "Bride-bride"[^3] was peculiar, but, postponing​ a pursue for it, Fuyuka tossed a question regardless:


"Erm, bride you say... What are you planning to do exactly?"
"What to do? Let's see, when it comes to being a bride, I shall be in charge of cooking, washing, cleaning, and so on; warming up the place to your, Hiiragi-sama, return! And of course, I shall as well take care of your personal needs, Hiiragi-sama! For that purpose, I've also duly studied about cosmetics and hairdressing, for instance."
"I see, I see..."


Apparently, Ohana's image of the bride tainted with slightly old-fashioned concept. Being that, however, for Fuyuka, whose employment taking considerable weight, the offer of  Ohana was pretty appealing.


Somehow or other, listening out Ohana's presentation, Fuyuka have seized her aim.<!--???-->
The degree of difficulty of sending her out like this is at its summit, too.<!--no too--> After all, no matter how one would look at it, her appearance is that of a child, she will be clueless of further actions after being driven out of here, and there is no guarantee she will not be assaulted by deviant, or be dragged into some kind of incident. If that occurs, Fuyuka  will doubtless be unable to sleep.
Thence, she has to ascertain what ensues in case of her refusal.


"So now, what if I refuse you?"
"Eh!? No, please don't!? What is my lack!? I'll correct it instantly; please name any thing which is to your discontent!"
"It's, it's different! I'm asking 'if,' only 'if!' Or rather, too close...!"


She managed to uncover her lips whilst restraining Ohana, who have been nearing  past the table, by both of the hands. Perceiving that, _Fum—_,[^5] after a moment of alert, girl replied.


"If that were to happen, my time will probably be passing at the Shinto shrine where I met you. For I have arrived here in favour of living together with you, Hiiragi-sama, I am incapable of returning until the curtain of your life shall fall."
"Er, eer... Is that certain?"
"Yes; for we are such family."


On the bright side, if Fuyuka face it outright, she will be accompanied by beautiful vixen for the remainder of her life.
To be honest, she pretty fond of her appearance: wholesome, spirited, and also earnestly devoted; possibly finest a category for spouse.
However, in view of social-scape, public opinions, and all the rest, she cannot be loyal to her desires.


Fuyuka was torn. Should she accept or reject her? Each comprise merits and demerits, and then again, probably for desires of Fuyuka were also blended amidst, she has no decision within her reach.
Amongst such an anguish, the voice of Ohana echoed gently:


"Hiiragi-sama, Hiiragi-sama, you are terribly forworn right now, are you not?"
"Eh?"
"I have a feeling it's like so. And withal, confused, for my visit is unforeseen, are you not...?"
"This... may be the case."


In fact, she was exhausted. She could have shattered by ill fortune and anxiety before long. Under such set of circumstances, it was sane that so suddenly chanced running into the vixen-maiden should render her bewildered.


"I, I assume it is appropriate to be upset by this sort of happening out of nowhere! Among us there were some who had been nigh executed after quarrels due to misunderstanding at  first interviews."
"Executed..."


Fuyuka was at loss, "When in the world did it happened?" Obviously, supposing she bore the potential of her execute, she would had no attitude of doing anything about this young and sweet creature.


"Hiiragi-sama, you should be the person of great kindness. Doubtless it is great mental exhaustion to involuntary protect, lending a hand to various a people, so I assume."


In spite of encounter had taken a place only few hours ago, Fuyuka was overwhelmed to find her own subconscious essence being read as a book.
She is,  as Ohana says, a soft-hearted. She is by no means a good person, however is the type who will be bothered, thinking "I have committed a blunder somewhere, have I not," even if, for example, the other party is at fault.


"That is the grounds of you listening to me, who have shown up with no warning, and not casting me out... I truly hanker to spend my time together with so kindhearted a lady."


Catching these words, Fuyuka sensed her heart ache. Never had anyone asked her afore in such a fashion, not since she became a cog in society.


"For it's only once, pray welcome me provisionally? And then, if it would be hopeless on no account, I shall take my leave. Would this be alright...?"


Ohana of as yet childish look bows her head profoundly. Fuyuka, too, recognised her apprehension, for her vixen's ears were slightly prone.


(Why in the world are you going so far... for something like me.)


Fuyuka does not consider herself an attractive person. As a matter of fact, she has a well-arranged features in her own way, and surroundings does not rate her that poorly, however, ignorant of it as she is, she undervalues herself.
She have been able, however, to partly calm down such a herself, hearing Ohana's proposal.
And despite how much of _Youko_ she was, despite how much a senior she was, throwing her out is unthinkable; painful so to imagine her, _potsun_,[^1] spending time desolate, all by herself at that shrine.


"...Understood. Then, let's call it provisional for now..."


Thence, she determined to follow suggestion of Ohana. Fuyuka was unsure of time span to be considered provisional, but she concluded "Surely she will drain her amiability and withdraw from here speedily, won't she."
The scars of her former life are hitherto lingered intimately within her heart.


"Wah—, thank you so much! I shall do my best to be your wife, please look forward to it!"


Fuyuka responded to miniature hand of Ohana, which was presented to her across the table, in a diffident manner. The feminine miniature but warm  hand felt both awkward[^2] and pleasant.

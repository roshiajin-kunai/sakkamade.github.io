Thus, past this and that, mysterious an act of the life of Fuyuka and Ohana have had its opening.
Today is Saturday, time is nigh upon five in the afternoon. Before long, average households  would start preparations for a dinner.


It was no exception to Ohana.


"So then, I shall definitely prepare the dinner enfolded in my love!"


Her ears are _byoko-byoko_,[^1] her tail is _bun-bun_,[^2] whereas Fuyuka blends in her sigh, and replies to the girl full of vigour.


"...I'm sorry, but I have no ingredients."
"He?"


Being allowed to open the fridge, Ohana, "Is this the fridge? It's much smaller than the ice house," murmured so, throwing it open and snapping her eyes wide.


"Err—..."


All that was enclosed within were but plastic bottle of tea and water, several eggs, and seasoning such as Vienna sausages of economical size and soy sauce.


"Pardon me, Hiiragi-sama, what do you commonly eat...?"
"A great deal of _bento_, I guess. I buy them at supermarket in front of the station or at convenience store nearby."
"H, how can it be..."<!--???-->


"There we go, you will be disappointment," puffed out her chest Fuyuka. Albeit it is hardly something to be proud of, if one lives alone, so is much simpler. When she just moved in, she tried her best at cookery, however the more she struggled amid the waves of society, the more she drifted away from cooking.


"Isn't this harmful for your health? When I studied, I was told that the _bento_ here are pernicious for health, for chemical seasonings which used for strong flavours."
"Perhaps you are right, but, well, it's simple and convenient. Or rather, I've been hearing the word 'study' for a while now, what kind of studying was that?"
"At place where I used to live, we have a custom of taking lessons upon the world one is marrying into. Therefore, I have thoroughly covered this side of the matters too!"


Fuyuka could not help feeling the information is over-weighted and incorrect, however decided not to delve into the matter, reaching a conclusion "I would supplement it if necessary."


"Currently, if we have no ingredients, we should go to purchase them! I shall put my best foot forwards! Do you have any requests?"
"Err—, isn't it okay for today? Let's start from tomorrow."
"B, but, for the sake of your health, Hiiragi-sama..."
"What reminds me, this 'sama', you needn't append it. This makes me somewhat uneasy,  I have never been called this way. Besides, even if provisional, shouldn't my wife call me more intimately?"


After such statement, pondering a little, Ohana nodded, consented as though.


"You are correct I imagine. Then what should I call you?"
"Just Fuyuka is fine. No honorifics are needed."
"Honorifics are mannerism of mine... Then, may I call you Fuyuka-san?"
"Um—, well I guess that's okay."
"Then, then, please call me Ohana!"<!--???-->
"Uh-huh, alright."


Said Fuyuka, arising.


"Now, let's head to the supermarket."
"Yes! We shall buy ingredients!"


Fuyuka, however, shook her head.


"No, we should have a _bento_ today."
"Eh? W, why? I'm fairly confident in my cooking skills..."
"Supermarket in front of the station is way better if we want to buy ingredients."
"...How is it better?"
"Supermarket behind apartments is small, has poor assortment, and prices are set a little too high. Moreover, they started to offer discounts on _bento_ and side dishes early on, so it's a good deal from a financial standpoint."
"H, haa—."
"Ohana, ingredients are not free. If you are able to buy them as cheaply as possible, you must do that, won't you agree?"
"That is quite so. However, please never mind the money!"


Declaring so, from out of nowhere Ohana drew a tiny cloth bag and tendered it to Fuyuka. Once received, it was much more weighty than she have expected it to be, and had almost dropped it.


"A, and this is?"
"The money! For it would be ill of me to have the one I have married with bear all the expenses, I brought my savings."


Fuyuka peeked at its content fearfully, finding the coins which one definitely might behold within a history textbooks.


"...Ohana."
"Yes, what is it! Ah,  Fuyuka-san, it's alright, you may avail yourself of them!"


Remarked Ohana, smiling broadly.


"Well, you know, this can't be used here."
"......Phuwhe—?"


"Haven't she studied it back there," wondered Fuyuka, as her shoulders slumped slightly; Ohana stay solidified with her lips parted, appearing to be perfectly astounded by it.

"I beg your pardon, I cannot believe it's of no use."
"Well, perhaps if we take it to the pawn shop, it might cost a great sum."


Fuyuka and Ohana were on their way to supermarket behind the apartments.
In the end, as Fuyuka shall be the one to provide the wherewithal, the pair settled on purchasing _bento_ for dinner today, and ingredients at supermarket in front of the station tomorrow.


"Ugh—, I am not worthy of being a wife, causing a distress whereas trying to be helpful..."
"Don't worry about it. You just have to learn this little by little. Besides, _bento_ isn't always a bad thing, why don't you try it today, Ohana?"
"...Alright. Today, I should prefer to experience this _bento box_, which is rumoured to be pernicious for health and rich in chemical seasonings!"
"......By the by, what is your favourite dish, Ohana? Sure enough, something between the _aburaage_[^3] or _inarizushi_?”[^4]
"Wah, amazing! How have you deduced it???"
"No, well, I thought it might be—."


This is a well-known fact that foxes inclined to prefer those things, and it appears to apply to Ohana either. "Would be nice to find a _bento_ with _inarizushi_," whilst thinking thusly, Fuyuka and Ohana proceed their walk.


"And what do you like, Fuyuka-san?"
"Me? Me, eh—, I wonder—. Honestly, any _bento_ is fine."
"Then, what would you want me to prepare?"
"Eh? Umm—..."


She has not had someone to cook for her in ages, which makes a reply baffling. Anything would be fine as long as it was prepared for her, however if she say so, Ohana would probably be stumped. Thence, presently, she chosen to answer what she liked in childhood.


"_Dashimaki tamago_;[^5] my mother used to make it once in a while. It was my favourite."
"I see, I see, I shall remember!"


She could not help a little smile at such remark of Ohana, who have threw her chest out. Supposing she prepare it, it would be positively joyful an event.<!--???-->
As they were having such a chatter, they arrived at supermarket. Albeit small, a lively one, for there are numerous houses round it. Everyone from children to elderlies who live in  vicinity  uses it.


"Let's enter then."
"Th, this is the supermarket. One may find all the daily supplies but ingredients herein."
"Though it depends on location."


Along with Ohana, who for some reason had been shivering with trepidation, Fuyuka stepped into the supermarket.

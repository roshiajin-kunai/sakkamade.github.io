Thus, past this and that, mysterious an act of the life of Fuyuka and Ohana have had its opening.
Today is Saturday, time is nigh upon five in the afternoon. Before long, an average households  would start preparations for dinner.


It is no exception to Ohana.


"So then, I shall prepare the dinner enfolded in my love!"
<!--excerpt-->

Her ears are _byoko-byoko_,[^1] her tail is _bun-bun_,[^2] whereas Fuyuka blends in a sigh, and replies to full of vigour girl.


"...I'm sorry, but I have no ingredients."
"He?"


Being allowed to open the fridge, Ohana murmured, "Is this a fridge? It's much smaller than the ice house," throwing it open, and snapping her eyes wide.


"Erm—..."


All its content comprised mere plastic bottles of tea and water, several eggs, and seasoning such as Vienna sausages of economical size and soy sauce.


"Pardon me, Hiiragi-sama, what is your common diet...?"
"Lots of _bento_, I guess. I buy them at supermarket in front of the station or at convenience store nearby."
"H, how can it be..."<!--???-->


"There we go, you are going to be disappointment," puffed out her chest Fuyuka. Albeit that is hardly something to be proud of, if one lives alone, so is much simpler. Just as she moved in, she tried her best at cooking, however the more she struggled amid the waves of society, the more away she drifted from cookery.


"Isn't this harmful for your health? When I studied, I was told that _bento_ here are pernicious for health, for chemical seasoning which is used for strong flavours."
"Perhaps you are right, but, well, it's simple and convenient. Or rather, I've been hearing the word 'study' for a while now, what kind of education was that?"
"At place of my previous reside, we have practice of taking lessons upon the world one is marrying into. Therefore, I have thoroughly covered this side of matters, too!"


Fuyuka could not help feeling the information is over-weighted and incorrect, however made up her mind not to delve into the matter, reaching a conclusion "I would supplement it, if necessary."


"Currently, if we have no raw ingredients, we need to go and purchase them! I shall put my best foot forwards! Do you have any requests?"
"Err—, isn't it okay for today? Let's begin it from tomorrow."
"B, but, for the sake of your health, Hiiragi-sama..."
"What reminds me, this 'sama', you don't need to append it. This makes me somewhat uneasy,  I have never been called this way. Besides, even if provisional, shouldn't my wife call me more intimately?"


After such statement, pondering a little, Ohana nodded, consented as though.


"I believe you are correct. Then what shall I call you?"
"Just Fuyuka is fine. No honorifics are needed as well."
"Honorifics are mannerism of mine... Then, may I call you Fuyuka-san?"
"Um—, well I guess that's okay."
"Then, then, pray call me Ohana!"<!--???-->
"Uh-huh, alright."


Said Fuyuka, arising.


"Now, let's head to the supermarket."
"Yes! We shall buy ingredients!"


Fuyuka, however, shook her head.


"No, we will have _bento_ today."
"Eh? W, why? I'm fairly confident in my cooking skills..."
"Supermarket in front of the station is way better to buy raw ingredients."
"...How is it better?"
"Supermarket behind the apartments is small, has poor assortment, and prices  are set a little too high. Moreover, here they started to offer discounts on _bento_ and side dishes early on, it's a good deal from financial standpoint."
"H, haa—."
"Ohana, ingredients are not free. If you can buy them as cheaply as possible, you must do that, will you agree?"
"That is quite so. However, pray never mind the money!"


Declaring so, from out of nowhere Ohana drew a tiny cloth bag, and tendered it to Fuyuka. Once received, it was  weightier than she expected it to be, and almost dropped it.


"A, and this is?"
"The money! For it would be ill of me to lay all the expenses upon the one I marry with, I brought my savings."


Fuyuka peeked at its content fearfully, finding the coins which one definitely might behold within history textbooks.


"...Ohana."
"Yes, what is it! Ah,  Fuyuka-san, it's alright, you may avail yourself of them!"


Remarked Ohana, smiling broadly.


"Well, you know, this can't be used here."
"......Phuwhe—?"


"Haven't she studied it back there," wondered Fuyuka as her shoulders slumped slightly; Ohana has been frozen in place, with her lips parted, appearing to be perfectly astounded by the matter.

"I beg your pardon, I cannot believe it's of no use."
"Well, perhaps if we take it to the pawn shop, it may cost a great sum."


Fuyuka and Ohana were on their way to supermarket behind the apartments.
In the end, as Fuyuka shall provide the wherewithal, the pair settled on purchasing _bento_ for dinner today, and raw ingredients at supermarket in front of the station tomorrow.


"Ugh—, what a wife I am... to cause a distress whereas trying to be helpful..."
"Forget about it. You  have only to learn this little by little. Besides, _bento_ isn't always so bad, why don't you try it today, Ohana?"
"...Alright. Today, I should prefer to experience this _bento box_, which is rumoured to be pernicious for health and rich in chemical seasonings!"
"......By the way, what is your favourite dish, Ohana? Sure enough, something between the _Aburaage_[^3] and _Inarizushi_?”[^4]
"Uwa, amazing! How have you gleaned it???"
"No, well, I thought there are chances—."


This is a well-known fact that these meals are highly favoured by foxes, and it appears to apply to Ohana either. "Would be nice to find _bento_ with _Inarizushi_." Thinking thusly, Fuyuka and Ohana proceed their walk.


"And what do _you_ prefer, Fuyuka-san?"
"Me? Me, eh—, I wonder—. Honestly, any _bento_ is fine."
"Then, what would you fancy me to prepare?"
"Eh? Umm—..."


She has not had someone to cook for her in ages, which makes reply baffling. Anything would be fine as long as it was prepared for her, however if she say so, Ohana would probably be stumped. Hence, presently, she chose to answer what she was fond of in childhood.


"_Dashimaki tamago_;[^5] my mother used to make it once in a while. It was my favourite."
"I see, I see, I shall remember!"


She could not help a humble smile at such remark of Ohana, who threw her chest out. Supposing she prepare it, it would reliably be joyful event.<!--???-->
As they were having such a chatter, they arrived at supermarket. Albeit small, a lively one, for numerous houses dispersed round it. Everyone from children to elderlies living in  vicinity visits it.


"Let's enter then."
"Th, this is the supermarket. One may find all the daily supplies but ingredients herein."
"Though it depends on location."


Along with Ohana, who for some reason shivered with trepidation, Fuyuka stepped into the supermarket.

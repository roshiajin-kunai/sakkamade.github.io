Just as they set their feet in to the supermarket, peculiar to it cheesy tune poured into their ear. Yet, Fuyuka does not mind the tune that much.


"Ooh~."


_Kyoro-kyoro_,[^1] the face of Ohana was moving restlessly, likely each and every scene which catching her eye has been novel to her. The people about had also, _chira-chira_,[^2] destined glances for so atypical, suddenly emerged maiden in _kimono_; however the person in question seem to pay no regard whatsoever.
Ohana, obviously having her ears and tail concealed, directs her sparkling eyes to Fuyuka.


"So this is the supermarket! Seems like it has everything, just as I  learned!"
"This one is still quite small. Supermarket in front of the station we are going to tomorrow is five stories high and huge, you might be even more impressed."
"Five stories... This place appears to have only one floor, so it's five times as vast?"
"You are right. Well, because they do offer more than groceries. And we also have  to buy daily necessities for you, Ohana, and..."


Announcing that much, Fuyuka was startled to find herself unwittingly set to accommodate Ohana. This is but provisional situation, and the girl will  leave  (in all probability) in near future, hence getting excessively attached would result in getting wounded afterwards.


(Hmm, but it's bare minimum, at least this should be fine, right.)


The notion of allowing maiden to bear  hardships whereas they living together was  beyond Fuyuka.<!--???-->


"Then shall we hurry to the stand with these _bento!_ ”
"Sure; this way, _this_."


She retained and guided nearly proceeding in different direction Ohana. Somehow, all of a sudden, Fuyuka envied the girl who has both adult and childish sides to her.
Not before they approached the section of _bento_ and side dishes as Ohana once again cried in admiration.


"Fabulous! Such a variety of choice!"
"You may select whichever you like. But think twice if it's too expensive."


Fuyuka raised her eyebrows to the sight of black, solemn, plastic container labelled "Japanese Unajyu,"[^3] and to its price. Even if she _will_ pay high price, it would be only for regular _bento_ and side dishes.


"Ah—! I take this one!"


An abrupt shout broke in to such a train of her thought. With start, Fuyuka hurriedly glanced around. For it has been close of the day, the crowd was abundant, and whole of it has been drawn to the owner of the voice.


"W, wait, Ohana!"
"Omhm——"


Ohana's lips were covered without any further arguments: "What is it?" intently stares the girl.


"S, silly, don't yell in places like this!"
"Mmh, mmh~~mn, mn."


_Uh-huh uh-huh_, seeing her nodding her consent with blocked lips, Fuyuka withdrew her hand. Gazes of surroundings were concentrated  upon her no longer, thinking she is only some noisy child.


"E, excuse me. I am just so excited..."
"Hmm, please stay quiet in places like this..."


During such an exchange, Ohana made her mind on _Sukeroku sushi_. The set of _Inarizushi_ and _Norimaki sushi_; decision was drawn probably since it included her favourite dish.
Fuyuka obtained casual _Yakitori_ bowl, which contains a soft boiled egg within: one of her most favourite. And picked fried white fish as a side dish to the boot, for its appealing price.


"Let's go with this for today, we'll buy the rest tomorrow."
"Yes! Ah, I will carry the basket!"
"Ah, th, thank you."


She reflexively passed it over to the hand tendered forth. "Well, it shouldn't be heavy, and it's better than making her dispirited by my untactful decline," reckoned Fuyuka, viewing smiling face upon Ohana.


"My, helpin' out, ain't ya, how kind o' you. I'll put this one for you then! He'you go."


Furthermore, owing to that, lady clerk pasted a discount sticker for her, so the outcome was rather profitable.


(Nicely done, Ohana...)


"What is this paper patch?" had a curious look Ohana, whilst Fuyuka walked to cash register, admiring discount power of the other's.



After returning to apartment, they took a brief break, and just past six o'clock started to prepare dinner. Inasmuch as today, under definite reasons, Fuyuka had merely light breakfast along with neglected lunch, she was pretty hungry.


"Ohana, would it be okay for you to begin now as well?"
"Yes. As I mostly had a dinner close to six too, there is not a slightest issue."
"I see. Which reminds me, what is your daily rhythm like?"


They chatting while placed in microwave oven _betno_ were heating up.


"Let's see. At time of my living there, I woke up about six in the morning, cleansed myself, and then attended _Terakoya_[^5] until the noon. Whereupon I had a training as _Youko_, had a dinner towards six, and went to the bed commonly at nine... in the evening."
"Umm—, what a decent life."


Going to bed at nine might sound a little too early, but impression of so healthy skin, and the vigour probably due to such rhythm.


"And what does yours look like, Fuyuka-san?"
"Eh, mine? Let me see—..."


Bits by bits, Fuyuka also speaks up. She confessed that on weekdays and holidays she leads chaotic life rhythm and keeps late hours due to some overtimes, looking the smart phone or idly watching television.
Ohana was flabbergasted to learn about sloppier life than she contemplated it to be, turning still.


(How is it, this hopeless adult in all the light! Seems to me your affection have rather depleted.)


When it comes to working people, time management inevitably becomes arduous in various ways. Retain healthful life atone to which she had as a child is hard; still more when she lives alone.
Fuyuka was slightly curious about what expression would Ohana assume upon listening such a scenario, but as the other regained her self, she appeared to be oddly composed.


"So that's how it is, I see. Understood."
"Um?"


Fuyuka subconsciously perceived certain resolution, short to lurking desire, within the remark, however as precisely at the moment sound of microwave ending rang out, she decided to let no keen thought upon it.

Probably for trying on so many clothes afterwards, Ohana was greatly fatigued. From point of the girl who had worn as much as _kimono_ up until today, existence of western clothes seemed alien and uncanny.


"Furthermore, this cloth is so thin... I feel rather disturbed."


Since the beginning, Ohana fidgety held the train of the dress she fitted on. And up to now she appears nervous, burdening herself by change from bulky Japanese attire to thin contemporary garments.
In connection, for she had her own clothes vanish just like so, she exited the store like so, wearing purchased dress.
Fuyuka smiles and replies to visibly alarmed Ohana:


"You'll have to get accustomed to it. From now on, you are forbidden to wear clothes of any spiritual powers."
"E-Eeh!?"


From Fuyuka's view, the likelihood of her sudden posing nude in the street shall be noughted. If it came to that, the outcome would be no trivial incident.


(If that were to happen, it would be  far from police matter... We have neither residence's certificate nor insurance card, let alone the proof of her identity.)


Presently, foremost fear of Fuyuka's is that  under whatever pretext being requested for Ohana's identity. Ohana has no such thing in her possession, anyhow. At such occurrence, she cannot even measure the suspicion which might befall herself for providing girl with shelter.


(We have to avoid that much by all means...)


As before, Fuyuka shudder imagining​ the broadcast of her person being apprehended.


"What is it?"
"No, it's nothing... More importantly, you should do your best to get accustomed to it. For living here it is."
"I should, indeed. I do apologies, I have caused you inconvenience, I shall do my best! However... pray excuse me... you had to make all these purchases for my sake."


Ohana, then, gestured to bag she was keeping. The clothes, of course, were not the only articles of their buying, in addition to first dress, they also bought a pair of footwear, which made up the luggage considerable.


"It's fine. I have the money since I didn't spend any... That aside, I can carry them, too. All these clothes—I am sure it's heavy."
"I am alright! It was bought for me, I shall carry it myself."
"I hope that's okay. Let me know if it gets hard."
"Yes; thank you very much!"


Ohana of fatigued bearing from a moment ago has yet relighted her customary brightness.


"Come to think of it, we are coming up to lunchtime. Why don't we take the chance and dine out?"
"_Dine-out_?"
"Eating a meal at the outside. It's worth knowing, perhaps you will need it yourself in the future."
"Hee—? The lay-by[^1] as though?"
"Lay-by?"
"Yes: the stop at the ridge or at the mountain path where one may allow themself of a halt. One do pay to acquire dumplings, tea... Is it different?"
"Umm—, sounds different, and not so different... Well, we'll understand when we there, shall we."


The words of Ohana emerged in Fuyuka an image of something from a period drama. It occurred to Fuyuka, "Her values and knowledge based on old Japan, in the end."
If that is the case, "After all, I must teach her common sense of the modern era," once again recognised Fuyuka.


"And since we are at it, is there anything you would like, Ohana?"


Stepping into arcade near the station, one may find next to any kind of restaurant. "Most likely she'd prefer something of Japanese cuisine," contemplated Fuyuka; the request, however, which she received meant to sent her in utter daze.


"Um, I... I was hoping to taste the fare titled the 'Hamburger.'"
"Eh!? Hamburger!?"
"I-It's alright if I cannot! However, I have seen the promotion on Tivi, it looked so delicious."
"A-aw, that's what it was. You surprised me."


Fuyuka was startled by vocabulary she could never have allowed to even think she would ever hear. Although was convinced after hearing that the girl seemed to only grow curious after TV commercial.


"It's exactly in front of the station, let's go there then. Oh, but it comes with onions, is that alright?"
"Eh? I don't mind if I do. I have no particular preferences."
"I-I see. Indeed..."

Such apprehension ended up groundless. In her front was Ohana, deliciously munching on a large hamburger in her hands.
Girls visited  store named the "Queens Burger" which offer hamburgers of large size. Its voluminousness was evident for Ohana held it with both hands.


"'Delicious'?"
"Yes, it is! Although I taste it for the first time, it appears exceedingly flavourful!"
"Oh, I'm glad."


She could but notice how the other was unaccustomed to such eating, sauce had been all over the girl's lips; it appeared, however, enigmatically picturesque upon girl like Ohana.


"Look, it's all around your mouth."
"Mmh-nmh..."


For she held a hamburger with either hand, Fuyuka has but to wipe it there for her with a paper napkin.


"You can eat calmly. It doesn't bother me if you take your time."
"Y, yash, pardon me..."
"When you done, we'll go shopping and I explain to you, then, about the money here."

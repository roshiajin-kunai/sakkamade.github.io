The brain of Fuyuka have suspended itself. Apparently, amount of intelligence which she may accept as human was past tolerable.


"Well, err—...?"


The maiden who has bowed politely a moment ago, witness lady motionless, spoke to her in  bashful voice. However, as no reaction befallen her all the same, she grew anxious, and, _chon_,[^1] pinched the clothes of _miko_ by the side.<!--excerpt-->


"Per, perchance we are unseen to her?"


From under the mask was heard a gorgeous tone of priestess which was to answer to that voice of unrest.


"No, I fear this is not the case, for we are on this side. I dare believe her mind cannot appreciate so unexpected a turn of events."
"In, indeed... Then, what shall I do?"
"Let's see. For now I suppose the quickest would be to touch and see?"
"Understood!"


The maiden in white _kimono_ nods to the answer of _miko_, and, _tote-tote_,[^2] approaches Fuyuka in childish trot. _Miko_ had to flusteredly re-adjust an umbrella at a little unforeseen maneuver.


"Well—... Eh."


What maiden performed was elementary movement of gripping one of Fuyuka's downcast hands in both of hers. Perhaps for difference between an adult and a child, but the hand of Fuyuka could not altogether be enveloped in both hands of the petite maiden; however fruitfulness of that was remarkably exceptional:


"Hyaaa!?!??"


Immature shriek. And albeit maiden started at so sudden, so strange a voice, she did not withdraw her hands.


"Say, can you see me?"
"Eh? Eh? Wh, what? You—in pure white?"
"Yes! Dear me, I'm so glad—, I thought perhaps you couldn't perceive us."


_Phew_,  peeking from silk floss headdress, the countenance was one of relief, with seemingly suitable to the age smile directed towards Fuyuka.


"I introduced myself earlier: I am Ohana. May I ask for your name?"
"I, I am, Hiiragi... Fuyuka Hiiragi, but..."
"Hiiragi-sama, are you! I look forward to being of service from today on! Now then, now then, if you will be so good as to remove this headdress⸺"
"W, wait a moment, wait!?"


Somehow Fuyuka muddled through to interrupt the dialogue, which, _don-don_,[^3] was about to deliberately proceed upon its own path. Instantly, expression of the young maiden before her eyes have clouded anxiety, and though feeling guilty Fuyuka tossed her a doubt.


"Um... Eh, what? Bride? Whose???"
"Whose you say. It was settled, was it not, yours, Hiiragi-sama? Oh! C, could it be a nuance of wedding is vary here!?"
"No, here it also should hold an equal definition."



The maiden's uncertainty has been answered by priestess maintaining an umbrella. Once approached closer, her stature was clearly much taller than Fuyuka's, and the fox mask completed her intimidating air.


"Is that right... So—, I came here as your bride-sama, Hiiragi-sama. So much so. Is there any difficult part?"<!--???-->
"Th, there, right there!  The 'bride'... Do you know what it means!?"
"Ah, ahuh—... The connection where we should live supporting each other for a lifetime as spouses, is it not?"
"It is! Eh? Am I the odd one??? Huh—?"


Fuyuka, who had a question mark in boisterous dance over her head, _zui_,[^5] once again lay under the pressure of young maiden.


"May I ask you to kindly take and receive the headdress for now? Otherwise nothing would begin!"
"Y, yes? The headdress that covers you, this?"
"Why, that's it! Come now, come now, kindly!"


Momentum of the maiden who called herself Ohana was so excessively masterful that Fuyuka laid her hands afore could even duly ponder it over.
By design, to wear bridal headdress is to keep a visage of the bride from being seen by anyone apart from her partner. In other words, taking it off certainly betoken... Exactly.
Unable to dwell on such thought, however, Fuyuka makes no pause and unhurriedly removes the silk floss headdress.


"Ears!? Why!?"


Since a while ago Fuyuka issued but daze and confusion. It was inevitable, however, for, _bounce_, from the head of the maiden before her eyes popped up a pair of  ears of the fox. "Some sort of accessory?" she thought to herself; but  they were  moving  naturally, and did whatsoever not appeared to be artificial articles.
Nevertheless, amazement of Fuyuka does not cease there.


"Tail!? Why!?"


Until a while ago she failed to note it due to confusion, or was rather conscious of it upon her approach, anyhow from about coccyx, passed through the white _kimono_, was wiggling a single bushy tail.
Ohana replies quizzically to revealing pure wonder Fuyuka.


"Are you actually surprised so much? It is commonplace, for I am of _Youko_ family..."
"_Yo, Yo-Yo-Yo-Youko_...???"


Once again, the capacity of the brain was exceeded by overwhelming information, and vertigo seized Fuyuka, when, at last, she was about to collapse to the ground.


"Be careful!"


The support came from the maiden before her eyes. In spite of difference between an adult and a child, Fuyuka was surprised to find herself being unexpectedly firmly supported, and simultaneously enfolded in mild a sensation of female which she has not experienced in a long time.


"Are, are you alright?"
"I am sorry... Everything is happening so fast, I can't seem to keep up... Huh?"


Hardly Fuyuka mustered herself to somewhat raise her bearing from supported position as the countenance have overcome a surprise.
Inasmuch as bizarre landscape from a moment ago restored its former shape. No row of numerous small _torii_ gates, no august Shinto shrine, and then no female  wearing a fox mask and _miko_ outfit was remaining.
However, singular vixen before her eyes—Ohana—stay invariable.


"It appears I was able to transfer safely..."


Whereas blinking at Ohana, who, _Phew_, have grown a sigh of relief, Fuyuka reached her hands to soft cheeks of the maiden.


"...Um? What is it?"


Whereupon, with no hesitation...


"Fuwhai—, whaiaayoudwooin~~."


_Mochi-mochi_,[^4] the cheeks were stretched as vinylon. So soft as _mochi_[^6] cheeks were not an apparition of any sort, and protest arose from the lips of Ohana.
Then, not sooner than Fuyuka took her hands away, she pinched her own cheeks next. Stretching them not so gently as earlier, it was rather painful.


"It hurts."
"Are you alright...?"


Ohana was puzzled by an abrupt conducts. Fuyuka, however, on the other hand, has been  baffled far beyond that.


"A, anyway, I am looking forward to be of service from now on!"


Fuyuka had to pinch her cheeks again at the words which as though proclaimed the beginning anew.

"I'm off—..."
"Yes, have a nice day!"


Without a promise of concealing her horribly dull shape, Fuyuka stepped out of the house. To no wonder, for it is a weekday and she has a job, but the sight has been sombre.


Ohana took pity on her, although all she could was to send her off with a smile. "So shameful of me," she thought also; but in fact, seeing her off cheerfully was the best she could do.


"Come now, Fuyuka-san is doing her best either, I shall do what I must!"


It has been three months  since Ohana has moved into Fuyuka's apartment, and  summer will shortly be here.


◇


The weekday activities of Ohana have grown more or less routine. First things first. Cleaning.


"There we go, phew..."


After removing dust with the broom, she took a dust cloth and polished corners of the flat, whereafter wiped it dry with a rag. There are some unused cleaning tools purchased by lady, but Ohana is more accustomed to former method.


"This should be enough for now."


Early summer has arrived, temperature is rising, and as a result Ohana was constantly drenched in sweat whenever she finished cleaning. As one would expect, for it is uncomfortable to remain as is, she would remove her sweat-soaked clothes and wipe her body with towel.


_"Take it easy! You can use cooler and anything you need, okay!?"_


Earlier, when Fuyuka heard how Ohana had not used conditioning system during the day, she cried so in panic. Ohana has no insurance card, something like heat stroke expected to be a disaster, and anxiety overcame lady all the more.


_"In this case, I shall put it on as the heat comes."_
_"It's a promise, okay?"_


Therefore, Ohana gave the lady her word to put it on during hottest hours of the day. Because Ohana also understands that, as Fuyuka say, she cannot be treated in hospital.


"I am off."


Thusly, after cleaning, she goes on her way to supermarket to do some shopping next. For dinner of course.
She changed into the clothes she accepted from Fuyuka, concealed her tail and ears, parted with lodgings, and went outside into the sultry heat.


During weekday hours, there were concerns regarding her being whisked into protective custody whilst she walk round with her child's appearance, however  her shopping has hitherto gone without hardships. Perchance for she buys vegetables, meat, and such, rather than sweets and juices.
Supermarket is well-cooled within, and is refreshing and pleasant after coming in from the hot without. In such a midst, Ohana paraded about the food section.


"What shall we serve today... It is hot, perhaps something easy to eat as _somen_ noodles."


Recently, as a product of these hot days, Fuyuka often comes home with little appetite. Therefore, cold dishes including _somen_ noodles and _hiyashi chuka_[^1] took a leading part in menu. Upon brief hesitation, she decided on _somen_ and simple _tempura_, and, filling the basket with ingredients required for the task, headed for self-checkout counter.


"I should think this is well within our budget!"


She is now completely comfortable with self-checkout system, although it took some time to get accustomed to first. Nevertheless, it might prove to be slightly difficult, depending on the amount of items she buy. Whether _Youko_ or  human, it is tough to carry a heavy bag on the hot way home


"I'm back—."


Returned to lodgings using her duplicate key, Ohana sorted out the objects of her trip, and then turned the air conditioner on. She, naturally, bathed in sweat, and whilst the chamber grew cooler, she took a shower to wash off the perspiration.
Her tail demand quite a while to dry off, so she winded it up with a towel, and relaxed for some moments with the cooler working.


"It is not noon yet, withal why not I eat a little earlier."


Usually, after cleaning and shopping, the time is  just before noon. For breakfast, she would prepare meal for Fuyuka, so they might eat it together, however in the daytime, she skimps it slightly, she needs but for oneself after all.
Assuming there are any leftovers from breakfast, she might add something to them—might buy some side dish available at the supermarket later. Yes, for example...


"W-well, this _inarizushi_ was cheap, I had no choice..."


Ohana loves _aburaage_. And of course, _inarizushi_ comprising it is her personal favourite; it is commonly mixed in with her lunch.
Such was her way of indulging herself. The day she shared this with Fuyuka, "You should be more extravagant," was the reply she received.


"Although I consider it a luxury to be able to eat _inarizushi_ whenever you want. Mmm, delicious!"


Ohana spent satisfying lunch, stuffing her cheeks by her tiny lips with _inarizushi_ which she bought.<!--???-->


"What a wonderful meal it was."


Once the tableware is washed, the afternoon comes, and from here on, here comes awhile of idleness.
The tasks of Ohana are cleaning, shopping, cooking and washing. Being that the laundry was done along with the cleaning, all she has is the evening cooking.
Then, even if she were to set off preparations for this, in light of the hours Fuyuka would be back, too much a time should lapse.


"Fwah-ha—..."


At such moments, she tend to consume her time watching television or taking nap when she start to feel sleepy. Today it happened to be the latter.


"In the end, waking up early in the morning... does make me sleepy..."


After lunch, what is more, after noon, is a perfect time to grow sleepy. Lying down upon the futon in which she always sleep together with Fuyuka, Ohana  curled herself up adroitly.


"Until dinner preparations... Good night."


Not before setting alarm clock and timer on the cooler, Ohana gently closed her eyes. So passes the first half of her day.<!--???-->

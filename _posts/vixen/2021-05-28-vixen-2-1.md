"Good morning~."


After Fuyuka's greeting, having already arrived to the office, several people returned to her a faint replies. Thusly, she seated herself at the desk and turned on the computer.
<!--excerpt-->

"Good morning, Hiiragi-san—."
"Oh, morning."


Motokane Kana, two years with the company, addressed Fuyuka.
When she joined the company, Fuyuka was her mentor and keeper, then, two years later, they remained in this same kind of relationship.


"Eh? new girls aren't here yet?"
"Hmm, I thought they supposed to be training for business etiquette elsewhere  all this week. Aren't they should be here only from next week—?"


It is mid-April. Not long before there was an initiation ceremony for fresh employees.


"I remember going through one as I just joined, too, but I much wonder if it has any point."
"I don't know—. I was all just 'How lucky~, no need to work but still receiving my salary.'"
"How honest. Well, it's going to be hell from next week, hopefully they enjoying the moment."
"You make it sound like it's their last supper before the campaign."


"Hopefully they would not throw in a towel, and _will_ start to work at the head office from next week on," was anxious Fuyuka. She herself has grown accustomed  to this rather exploitative tendency by now.<!--???-->
Whereas she was occupied by thinking of new employees undergoing such training, Kana abruptly went for distinct topic.


"Incidentally, Hiiragi-san, did something happen?"
"Eh!? E, excuse me?"
"I don't know, though; on Mondays you always have a look like you're about to die any moment soon, but today you relatively normal, ish."
"N, no~? Not really~?"
"Secrecy is not your strong point, is it. Well, I suppose it's much better than being in doldrums, though you've got me curious~..."

"U, ugh."


Fuyuka could not but back away as her junior futilely flashed  her a charming​ eyes. Thusly, then, her mind drifted backwards into today's early morning.

"Good morning Fuyuka-san. It's morning."
"Um, mhm..."


Fuyuka leisurely travel her eyes ajar to yet unaccustomed voice, and to gentle shaking, to discern Ohana who was peering in from above.


"Whue!?"


All of a sudden, gorgeous vixen had filled Fuyuka's sight, causing her to rapidly bring her consciousness back, and to even spring up to her feet.


"Oh, Oh, Ohana...!?"
"Yes: it is me... The breakfast is ready."


If one were to peep at the clock, it was half past six. For usual Fuyuka, it is common to wake at seven, then to, _dara-dara_,[^1] slothfully make herself ready, and to depart just like that.


"Now pray come and wash your face. I shall arrange dishes in meanwhile."
"Ah, uh-huh..."


"When did she wake up?" Similar to their first night, they shared the futon last night likewise, hence to notice her awakening expected to be plain; but perhaps Fuyuka had been too deep in her sleep to perceive. She, of course, went to bed before nine o'clock, and does not have usual  languid drowsiness.
By Ohana's urging, Fuyuka washed her face and brushed her teeth, and the breakfast was already arranged upon a low _chabudai_[^2] by the time of her return.


"After your said you have no habit of breakfast, I  made a light meals today."


Rice with miso soup and omelette with _tsukemono_[^3] lay upon the table. Either of them in rather smaller servings than in the evening, and could be eaten quickly.


"Ohana, what time did you get up?"
"I did at six. I spent yesterday's evening on little preparations, so this much went briskly! Heh-heh!"
"Amazing... seriously."


Even getting up earlier than usual she had hardly had time to spare, so they were deprived the room for chat. Fuyuka takes her seat at the table, after all dishes were prepared for her through such a great effort. Ohana has also similarly seated herself down, and began munching together with her.


"Ah, this is _Dashimaki_..."


Once omelette came into her mouth, the taste of the _Dashi_[^4] gently spread within. It was Fuyuka's favourite flavour.


"Yes, I made it since it's your favourite. These supermarkets sure are wonderful, they have all sorts of flavouring for _Dashi_."
"If I had to say, Ohana, it's you who can prepare it within a twinkle is  wonderful one."


Fuyuka was positively conscious that they bought some kind of seasonings on occasion of visiting supermarket. At the time, she merely passed it off as okay, by no means  could she expect to be buying them for _Dashimaki_-business right away.


"Does it suit your taste? I have sampled it, and I don't find it insipid."
"Uh-huh, it's very delicious. Just the way I like."
"Is that right! I am so glad to hear!"


Fuyuka should normally be too drowsy to have an appetite in the morning; however she found herself absorbing it in no time at all, and in fact, was a little dissatisfied.


"...I'm off."
"Yes! Have a good day!"
"Say,  yesterday we discussed the daytime and my late return, so..."
"I know, I know! Pray go to your work and don't let you mind dwell upon me! If you are tardy, you shall be scolded by all _nechi-nechi_[^5] and fussy voice of that raised Director-san, shall you not?"
"..."


Regardless that it was Fuyuka who enlightened her such an intelligence, to have the girl recite it again therein was something which made her somewhat uncomfortable. However, as it was the truth to a certain extent, at that time, Fuyuka determined to silently leave the house.

(I hope she is alright.)


Fuyuka persistently warned the girl not to open the door if someone comes. Her apartment might be inhabited by the pair of people as long as that be talked through, although is basically for single person's use.
Hence, she has also told the girl not to go out unprepared. Of course, for she cannot for ever stay confined at lodgings during all weekdays, the pair means to settle thereabouts in the next days off.


(...I'm worried.)


None the less, for Fuyuka, the idea of young girl to remain alone, enclosed with the walls of the house, was hitherto nothing but the point of agitation.

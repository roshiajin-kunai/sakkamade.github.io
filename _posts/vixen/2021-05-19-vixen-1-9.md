Evidently, Ohana was  pleased with _Sukeroku sushi_ from supermarket.
The air of her tail flutter from base to tip, and her relaxed cheeks during eating  had naturally soothed Fuyuka's heart, regardless of whether she was _Youko_ or not.


"I was sceptical, but it is fairly good."
"From whose perspective is this commentary? But I guess you understand it now. Supermarket's _bento_ are convenient."<!--???-->
"That is true, but... is every day like this?"
"Umm—, pretty much."


The image of Fuyuka who was doing her best to cook for herself at commencement of living on her own is nowhere to be found now. The solitary remnants left are a little dusty rice cooker and dish dryer.


"Certainly I understand, this is conveniently simple indeed. However, is not your nutrition unbalanced by doing this on daily basis? And together with life rhythm I have just heard of, this will not make your tiredness go..."


Fuyuka chuckles at worried Ohana, "This more like mother than wife—."


"It cannot be helped, however. For working people somehow built upon that sort of feeling. Probably."


In point of fact, Fuyuka was ever exhausting herself, either physically and mentally.
For even in prior of  various mishaps she has been so occupied by her work  schedule that her life rhythm was withered, on days off she slept and slept and slept without a shred of fulfilment, she kept unbalanced diet, was stressed, and so on, and so on... At any rate, her fatigue is unlikely to ever subside.
Fuyuka smiles to herself a bit wickedly, whilst immersed in such sense not unlike resignation, as she reflects upon her miserable life.


"So, Ohana, what will you do for this completely worthless me, I wonder? You are my wife, aren't you."
"Mm-hmm—..."


Enquired such, Ohana began to gravely brood over it. "Sleeping hours, nothing of domestic chores, diet... However, fundamentally..."  so she started to murmur, and does not quite seem to return.
Her striking tail kept swaying itself calmly from side to side. "Honestly I want to stroke her ears and tail somehow," Fuyuka thought, for she loves animals very much.


"Um—, sure enough the burden of housework is hea...vy. What is it?"


Ohana asked quizzically, noting a look with mixture of ardent desire in the eyes of the lady. Albeit she did not appear to pass so far as to reveal Fuyuka's thought; but merely inclined her head sideways, setting afloat question mark.


"No, never mind... S, so then, what is your action plan?"


Fuyuka hastily redirects the subject to disguise the fact that she was staring too hard. Ohana, however, did not seem to particularly mind, and replies to the question docilely.


"For example, I could take care of house chores such as daily cooking and laundry! From pondering about what I heard earlier, it seems that you have grown worn out badly from work to place your spirits into other matters."
"I would certainly be delighted with this, but..."
"Therefore, if I fill in your everyday's housework, you shall have slightly more leeway, shall you not? As the one who have come to marry you, I should be glad to be allowed to do at least this much."
"Ohana, I don't really want you to serve as my housekeeper, how should I put it, I feel our concepts of the 'wife' are a little unalike—."


The wife Ohana refers to is of slightly outdated notion.
Tenet, so to speak, of taking a step backwards and donating fervent assistance to the partner from the shadow, which is fairly different from world's model of this day and age.
Albeit Fuyuka does not wish to let Ohana bear all the housework too, so that she could concentrate on her job. Her ideal scenario is mutual support in such doings, if possible.
Ohana nodded her assent as well:


"Neither am I thinking of being housekeeper, though? My only wish is to do what I can, and if you dissent from leaving everything up to me, why don't we divide the workload between each other?"
"Mm-hmm—..."


This time it was Fuyuka's turn to hum in agreement.
The point of Ohana is not incorrect, and depending on wording, it is close to ideal shape for Fuyuka.


Ultimately, in view of the fact that she had met the girl only today, and had determined to admit her as the wife, even if temporary, presently she settled to honour judgment of Ohana.


"Alright... For now, I'll merely ask you to stay within a reason then."
"Yes!"


Fuyuka breathed a sigh at  joyfully replying, enthusiastic Ohana, and glanced at timepiece in the room.


"Only seven—."


After finishing _bento_ and washing containers, they had a moment of peace and tranquility. It was still too early to prepare for bathing, however, on the other hand, it hardly signified lack of questions at hand.


"Come to think of it, Ohana, you've said that you go to bed at nine?"
"Yes, I have. Ah, it's alright, you need not adjust to me."
"No, well, I think it's good to have an early night once in a while. But problem is not there..."
"Eh?"
"I have only one set of futons."


Remark had momentarily enwrapped the chamber in silence.


"H, haa—, I see."


But Ohana shot these words wearing a countenance implying nuance of "What is  she saying?"


"'I see?' And how were you planning to sleep!?"
"How? We shall sleep together, shall we not? If so, shouldn't it be fine to share the same futon?"
"Eh, eeh!? Together!?"


Though Ohana is _Youko_ her appearance as yet of the maiden, and as for Fuyuka there was not willing in her to enter the bedding together.
As mentioned previously, Fuyuka view the same sex as an object of romantic interest to the point of dating a female (albeit forlorn by now). Of course she loves charming ladies and pretty girls.
Thence, in such activity as sleeping together with Ohana, who has ears and tail, but plainly a beautiful girl, it is obscure if lady would be able to maintain decent mental state.


"W, wait. No matter how you put it, the day of our encounter is not the day... of becoming newly-weds—— We are newly-weds!"<!--???-->


Therein was consent on provisional situation, however was not restriction on sleeping together, and Ohana had intended to do so from the very beginning, in the first place. Therein was the pretext behind her complete absence of panic.


"Fear not! I have diligently, sufficiently practised, and pray entrust me with bathing as well!"
"Practised!? Practised what!? What were you doing!? Or rather, taking a bath  together too!? No—impossible—no!! It's small, it's too small—!"


The evening passes noisily.


Bathrooms in economic apartments are quite confined, furthermore they are unit baths. If two people enter together, ending up in some sort of cuddle is inevitable. As one would expect, Fuyuka had to prevent them from bathing together, because doing this nude truly unfolds a rich variety of risks.


"Not too cramped?"
"No, it's alright. Alright..."


However in return, they shared a bed. Or rather, there was but one set of futons, and Fuyuka could do nothing against Ohana's saying, "If we cannot sleep together, the floor shall be my bed."
It was a bit forced, but letting the girl who came to be her wife to sleep directly upon wooden flooring would be too heartless for Fuyuka.
Through this and that, now they are together under the covers.


"Fwa-ah—..."


Ohana is petite. Leaving out of consideration the height of her ears, she is probably about the height of middle school pupil.
As a matter of habit, little past nine, she immediately began to nod, and naturally nestling up to Fuyuka, she closed her eyes.


(Stay calm; stay calm; stay calm——)


Setting aside her look of middle school pupil, she was beautiful girl, appending to it her so excessively defenceless a state, and Fuyuka had to desperately withstand the urge of reaching out for her. Her voluminous tail and occasionally twitching ears were terribly attractive.
Fuyuka, whose desire was nigh to leak from alien to her warmth of the body by the side, frantically shut her eyes, forcing her consciousness away.


(Evil spirits, go away; evil spirits, go away; evil spirits, go away——)


A struggle between Fuyuka and her appetite for flesh  lasted throughout the night within her head as she chanted an unintelligible words over and over again.

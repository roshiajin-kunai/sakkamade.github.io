Sunday. Fuyuka, who had to be reliant on meals, in conclusion, called today a day of rest.
Specifically, a day of mere lounging about at home.


"Say—."
"Yes?"


Past one o'clock on a calm afternoon, after having lunch, a relaxing hour was there. It was in the midst of it when Fuyuka spoke to the girl.


"Do you have any hobbies, Ohana?"
"Eh? Hobbies, is it? Um~."


Up until now, Fuyuka's holidays have been an extremely listless days. The days where she would wake up before lunch whereafter do nothing whatever to lessen her fatigue.
First she thought of spending today blankly, too, without doing anything to soothe her tired mind from work; the presence of vixen-girl, however, whom she shared the chamber with, made her feel rather fidgety. For that reason, without knowing why, she spoke to her.


"My hobby, I guess, would be household chores."
"Chores... Anything else?"
"Eh? U-um~."


For Fuyuka's part, she felt ever so intolerable to have Ohana in the middle of cleaning whereas she herself lay spiritless. After all, how could she permit her junior (in appearance) do that whilst she positioned upon the futon? How hideous would she look in the public eye.


(Well, it's not as if public knows about us, we don't need to be bothered by it much...)


The fluffy tail wagged as Ohana was pondering over it, but apparently, she cannot come upon nothing but domestic tasks. "No matter how you look at it, there sure is something else," expected Fuyuka; but she say to have continuously lived this way, and it appears to have perfectly ingrained within her.


"I've been wondering, how is your family now?"
"Fami...ly?"
"Uh-huh, parents that is..."
"Oh, I have no parents."
"...Eh."


Although it should be an innocuous question, Fuyuka froze at the reply to it. Spotting that, Ohana hurried to correct herself.


"I never knew, for I have been under Shi Ren-sama's care for as long as I can remember."


She sounds as though this is commonplace in their homeland, she was not even sure whether her parents ever existed, in the first place. "That is what _youko_[^1] are," concluded she; however, Fuyuka hitherto cannot bring herself to admit it.
Now, content she or not, there is nothing that can be done about it.


"One may call it 'Mysterious circumstances', eh..."<!--???-->
"Fuyuka-san, you need not upsetting yourself of it at all!"
"Okay. Let's leave it aside for now... I have yet another point I'd like to bring up. Ohana, you've come to be my wife, have you?"
"Yes, I have, yes..."


Wife... Albeit they might hold divergent values of being one, it comes from Ohana. And in the end, even if they are members of the same sex, they should plainly be considered a couple.


"Th-then, if, if—for example—there is something I want to do to you, Ohana, I can...?"
"...Something you want to do... to me...?"


Ohana blushed a little, upon hearing it. She might look as a child, however her age was well above Fuyuka's, and she of course has such sort of knowledge.


"I have a favour I must ask of you..."
"I-if I may be so good to help it..."


Originally, Ohana intended to meet such requests as they were made.
Ohana swallowed a little. She could never had expected it to be this early in the day, but...


"Th-then, um..."


Slowly, Fuyuka drew closer to Ohana, and...

"Does..."
"Um-m-m—?"


The ridiculously weak, dumb voice come from the rear as Ohana addressed the other with annoyance.


"D-does it feel good? Doing this."<!--???-->
"To say the least, it's fantastic..."


Ohana was but baffled by Fuyuka's shameful appearance when she cuddled the fluffy tail.


_“I want to cling to your tail...”_


Was a favour which made Fuyuka with slightly coloured face, a few minutes ago. Ohana, who had been contemplating something else entirely, was partly dispirited; being that there was no particular reason to decline, however, she gave her approval.


"Hafwu—..."


For Fuyuka, the fluffy tail and the ears were the only targets of her desire to touch. Still more as the bearer was a beautiful girl.
During sleeping with Ohana, she would touch her lightly, however never was she able to cuddle with her for wishing to avoid awakening her.


"Mmhm."


When she caressed her tail while rubbing her cheek, Ohana would react with a twitch. She mentioned not being especially sensitive to it, but that did not mean she was free of sensation.


"Ah-umm."


Amongst humans who have unleashed their desires, seldom there ones who knows how to stop. Never noticing that Ohana trembled at times, Fuyuka continued to fondle her until she was satisfied.<!--???-->

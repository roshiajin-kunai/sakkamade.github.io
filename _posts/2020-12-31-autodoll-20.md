Lydia and Rapha were drinking café au lait​ in the dining room, at that time Yuuyami came in.


"Welcome back," greeted her Lydia.
"Yes. I am back," Yuuyami sat in the chair.


Before four-person table Rapha was siting in front of Lydia, whereas Yuuyami sat to Lydia's left side.


"Well then, let's drink it and return to the library."


Lydia dropped her gaze upon the cup with café au lait​.


"Indeed. I am interested to see progress of Maker Factory likewise."
"Before you'll go home, I have two stories I'd like to narrate you."
"Ah, I have something to ask Rapha either."
"If so, please go ahead, onee-chama."
"Um. Well, why are you calling me onee-chama, not onee-sama?"
"Is that what you wanted to hear? It's not a lead in?"


Frowned her eyebrows Rapha.


"Uh-huh. That's main question, you know?"


Staring at Rapha, Lydia tilts her head.
Rapha let out a deep sigh afore saying:


"It was a verbal slip up​. You don't remember, onee-chama, but I used to call you onee-sama, you know?"
"Is that so?"
"Yes, so it is. Once I called you *onee-chama* by mistake, but I was told  you pleased with it and I need to continue calling you onee-chama."
"I see. Thank you. Well then, please, Rapha's stories."


Upon understanding, Lydia smiled.


"The two stories of mine."
"I heard that a short while ago."
"Nm..." Rapha sent a bitter countenance. "Don't interrupt me. That just a  preface."
"Okay-"


Happily replied Lydia; Rapha sighed.


"The first one. The one regarding​ us."
"Is that about me and Rapha?"
"Yes, that's right. Onee-chama, please, be silent for a moment."
"Understood. I'll keep my mouth as tight as shellfish."


Lydia squeezed her lips with fingers of both hands.
And then, but I never seen a real shellfish, she added in her heart.


"As I already said before, we are the new creation of human beings. So, I will narrate you the matters that you surely concerned about."


Lydia nodded couple of times.


"First of all, our life span longer than of ordinary humans."
"Up to what extent is it?"


Rapha's words were snapped by Yuuyami.


"Well, average life expectancy​ of human beings was around one hundred fifty years, our is twice longer; thus it's about three hundred years."
"Three hundred years..."


Yuuyami turned her eyes down as she pondered over it.
For each person three hundred years may be considered as long or a short time.


"No more than that?"


Lydia believed it being short. That was insufficient for world exploration together with Yuuyami.
Until now, Lydia was never actually concerned about how many years she would live.
Accordingly, she grew a little nervous to discover her exact finale.


"For now, let's say," _hmm_, inhaled Rapha. "I'm still continuing my research, so I can prolong​ it up to four or five hundred years."
"Rapha-san, please, allow me to assist, with that research."


Yuuyami directly gazed at Rapha.
Rapha showed a slightly surprised expression at such request, however in instant restored her usual look.


"Well, I'll pass the data to you. So, please, do it on your own. I don't want to collaborate."
"Understood. Thank you very much. However, in case of you getting any progress, could you share it with me?"
"If so, it's a contest, Yuuyami."
"Contest?"
"Yes, that's right. The contest, which of us will achieve better result."
"Ha-..."
"Do your best, Yuuyami."


Lydia tapped Yuuyami's shoulder.


"Wah, how crafty! Onee-chama is forbidden to contribute her! Cheering​ is forbidden too!"
"Eh, but, I wanted to tell Rapha to do her best either-"
"I will allow you rooting for me."
"Ah, uh-huh. Do your best, Rapha."
"Yes, I will!"


In delight smiled Rapha.
Desire​ to conquer Yuuyami; Lydia understands this feeling likewise.


"Due to discussion on the subject regarding reporting each others' outcome has been settled, please let us move to the  second subject."
"There's more, you know?"
"Is that so? I shall apologise."


Yuuyami slightly bowed her head.
With light nod, Rapha continued.


"We are not able to grow any more."
"Eh?"


Put her hands on the chest Lydia.


"Did you noticed that only now?"


Seeing Lydia's movement, Rapha asked that.


"Oh no, I, haven't thought about it, but-"


Lydia height does not progressed in a long time. Along with no changes can be seen in her physique. However, she haven't worried about it. But believed she would  grow greatly someday.


"_Oh no_?"


A little tilted her head Rapha.


"To think that my breasts wouldn't grow-, I was rather shocked."
"Th, that's right... W, well-, no need to worry. I'm investigating this problem too, okay?"


Rapha also looked down on her chest.
There was a precipitous cliff. However, Rapha ought to look that way,  judged Lydia.
Nevertheless, she didn't say it. There being possibility to fly these words back to herself as a boomerang.
Rapha sent a glance at Yuuyami.
However, Yuuyami does not say a thing.
Brief silence.
The air is delicate. Impossible to describe as comfortable. Still, that doesn't mean it was heavy either.
Lydia forced a wry smile; Rapha let out light sigh.


"Yuuyami," says Rapha. "Do you need the data?"
"Yes. There is no particular problem with it."
"It's serious!" "Wah, it's serious!"


Replies Lydia and Rapha against Yuuyami's remark, overlapped.


"However, that is not concerning a life. Life expectancy being a direct subject of life span."


Indifferently said Yuuyami.


"AD cannot understand it..." Rapha looked at Lydia. "I'm not making a fun of Yuuyami, okay? I've always been respectful. It's just insurmountable wall due to differences of species..."
"I know."


Lydia interrupted Rapha's words afore slightly shrugging her shoulders.
That was common for Lydia to have disagreements with Yuuyami. Moreover​, she recognised Rapha treating Yuuyami as an intelligent life-form.
Otherwise, the word *contest* wouldn't appeared.


"So you don't have to look at me like that?"
"I, I'm not particularly looking at onee-chama."
"Is that so?"
"So that is."


*Humph*, turned away Rapha.


"Um," says Yuuyami. "My development also... No, my height does not changing, however it is not particular issue, right?"
"It's not about height."
"Then, what are we talking about?"
"Try to guess."
"Hmm."


Yuuyami showed a thoughtful gesture. Probably reviewing the action log.


"Indeed. Breasts, right. Lydia's statement that she was shocked her chest wouldn't grow, and since then subject being regarding the breasts, right?
"...Yuuyami," said Lydia with a wry smile. "If you guessed, you don't have to utter, alright?"
"Is that so? I shall remember."
"Please do. Incidentally, Rapha, is that topic continuing yet?"
"No, it's not. I'll quickly move to the second one."
"Uh-huh. Let's do it."


Nodded Lydia.


"I'll cut to the gist, we have another little sister."
"Eh?"


Lydia shoot her eyes open.
What  will I do if she would be same type as Rapha, wondered Lydia.
Single complicated, mysterious, and troublesome little sister, was already enough.


"Name is Michael. Onee-chama and I used to call her Mika. So, that  Mika, I think,  brought this world to such a state."
"Do you mean, she destroyed the world?"


Asked Yuuyami.


"I don't know. Still, surely Mika was involved. Besides, onee-chama, possibly, may be a little implicated too."
"I, am?"


Lydia intertwined her arm with Yuuyami's. Otherwise, felt as though would collapse.
She may have wrecked majority of lives. It was as yet upon a phase of possibility, however Lydia grew very anxious.
Lydia has a plenty of knowledge. And then, got beloved partner. Accordingly, Lydia understands. Loved companion was within each of others' life.


"It will be alright," says Yuuyami. "Present Lydia is different person from the former​ Lydia."
"That's right," agreed Rapha. "Besides, I don't know if onee-chama was really involved. But, at the very least, onee-chama was aware that world will be in such a condition. From that, you might have been involved a little, is only my guess."
"Yes, right."


The answer to both, Yuuyami and Rapha.
Even so, Lydia's mind was not clear. It being somewhat gloomy; an overcast as though.


"Can I ask you for farther details?"


Yuuyami was indifferent as ever.


"Fine. But, I wasn't on good terms with Mika too. So, it's fragments of pieces of information."
"That is not a problem. Please."
"Onee-chama, are you alright? You don't seem to look well."
"Uh-huh. I'm fine."


So long as was holding on to Yuuyami's arm.
Rapha exhaled a little, and began:


"Mika had no distinction between right and wrong."

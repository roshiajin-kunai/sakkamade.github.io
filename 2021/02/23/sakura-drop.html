The voice of Yoshino made my heart palpitate so fast that I wanted to scream. My ears were occupied by an irregular chaos of my heartbeat.

“Would you like a lemon drop?”

I nodded greatly, she, then, placed the lemon drop by a hand in her mouth and drew my chin towards herself.
The solid texture of the lemon drop kept passing back and forth, enclosed within our mouths… The sugary taste and pink fragrance, similar to blossoms of sakura, kept its spread more and more amidst our mouths… resulting in a full bloom.

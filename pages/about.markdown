<h3> Website </h3>

I begin translating inspired by reading a translations of Japanese webnovels.
Especially after reading the "[_Autodoll wa Kuchita Sekai de Yume wo Miru_](https://horobinomichi.wordpress.com/autodoll-wa-kuchita-sekai-de-yume-wo-miru/)". The novel seemed to be so good, I no matter what wished to write a comment with expression of my gratitude to translator.
Therefore, I created a profile on the *WordPress*, and, simultaneously, created a website either. Just for the sake of "why not?"

One month has passed... 
Honestly, all I wanted was to find the cover art to make a neat document with copy of that unfinished translation. (I've already experienced a situations when great blogs with translations were deleted, right before my eyes.) However, what I found instead was a website with originally published *webnovel* within. To tell you the truth, that inspired me greatly. Since I like writing and editing, after [two test-runs](https://sakkamade.wordpress.com/2020/10/), Tayaki's *Oneshots* (fantastic manga-artist; [here](https://www.pixiv.net/en/users/2239312) their *pixiv*), I've decide to give it a try.

<h3> My First Time </h3>

With *manga* I became familiar not a long ago. That was, if I recall currectly, in August of 2019. And the name of it - "Citrus." Finding it after casual encounter with some fan-art, firstly I watched an *anime*. At that time, my novice self didn't possessed a knowledge that all *anime* are based on *manga*. I presume that was a second *anime* I've ever seen in my entire life. Late bloomer, huh. Rather, I was never a favour of movies and the likes. 
That's remains as the one of my favourite *anime* and *manga* up until now. 
As not so long ago, at the very top came an *anime* "Liz to Aoi Tori." 
And *manga*... Hmm, I wonder, there were plenty of nice pieces.

The first novel I've read was "I Favor the Villainess." Best part I consider the story about Rei's past.
Found it in March of 2020 via fan-artwork in the translator's credits of some *manga*. Read it up to August of the same year (mainly in August, in couple of days.) <del>Gosh, what's wrong with this August.</del> And ever since then I've started to read much more novels and less *manga*. 
It's so hard to find an interesting one, gosh...
Favourite novel  is "Adachi to Shimamura." No doubt.

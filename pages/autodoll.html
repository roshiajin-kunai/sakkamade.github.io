Description

This is a story about the world after its perishing.
The girl have lost her memory after awakening from suspended animation, and lady, a female type of Autodoll, helped her. Autodoll told a girl that she was "exploring the world" while using an underground library as her base camp. ―"Would you like to become friends with me?"
Two young ladies who have nowhere to go, what truth about this world will be discovered by them..?

Original: オートドールは朽ちた世界で夢を見る (Ōtodōru wa Kuchita Sekai de Yume wo Miru)
Author: 葉月双 (Hazuki Sou)

Genre: Adventure, Android, Future, Post-Apocalypse, Shoujo Ai, Yuri; (With sexual depiction)

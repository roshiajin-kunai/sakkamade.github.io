Autodoll Dreams in a Decaying World

Foreword

This is a story about the world after its perishing.

The lady, a female type of Autodoll, had saved a little girl who have lost her memory after awakening from suspended animation. Autodoll, having performed a “world exploration” while using an underground library as a base camp, asked the girl:
“Would you like to become friends with me?”

Two young ladies with nowhere to go; what truth about this world should they discover…?

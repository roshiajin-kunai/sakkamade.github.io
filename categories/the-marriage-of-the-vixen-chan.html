The Marriage of the Vixen-chan

Summary

“I’ve come here to you to become your wife!”

The girl she was dating has rejected her in a way she could have never expected, and, what is more, her misfortunes continued throughout daily life, and at work.

That was when, in front of this exhausted to the core Fuyuka Hiiragi, put in an appearance a maiden with ears of vixen and a bushy tail who introduced herself as Ohana.

Despite being bewildered by girl’s claim of becoming her wife, Fuyuka gradually opens her heart to a cheerful, a little clumsy but tender towards her girl.

This story is a warm and gentle tale of a certain worn-out member of society and a vixen-girl who have come to her as her wife.

Original: お狐ちゃんのお嫁入り (O-Kitsune-chan no O-Yomeiri)
Writer: 熊煮 (Kumani)

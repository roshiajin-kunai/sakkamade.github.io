Description
(Beware, spoilers!)

A modern witch-story woven by a pair of witches with paradoxical personalities.

Being named a genius before, she have lost her magic and remembrance, and could no longer fly high up in the sky.

A girl, a student of “Tokyo University of Arts and Music” of an absurd personality—“Space Magician: Tsugumi,”

The one who have pursued her and arrived here, to Tokyo,
a bothersome girl, a slight stalker—“Witch of Justice: Rino.”

Whereas solving diverse a troubles that had its occur amid Tokyo,
two rugged individuals playing an active part in recovering a girl’s lost force.

As for the answer that the pair will choose, and the whereabouts of their love——
